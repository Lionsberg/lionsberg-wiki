# Circles

A **Circle** is the irreducible unit of the [[New Civilization]] — the living cell from which the entire organism grows.

It is not a committee, a task force, or a study group. It is a **complete, self-governing expression** of [[Heaven On Earth]] at human scale — a place where sovereign beings learn to love, trust, and co-create together.

Every great movement that has ever transformed a world began this way: small groups of committed souls, gathering regularly, doing real work, and multiplying through trust.

___

## Structure

| **Principle**          | **Specification**                                                                                                         |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Circle Size**        | 3 – 12 people (ideal 5-7)                                                                                                |
| **Composition**        | balanced by polarity, temperament, and gift; may mix households, vocations, ages                                          |
| **Covenant**           | one shared vow or set of simple agreements                                                                                |
| **Rhythm**             | meet weekly; monthly for synthesis; seasonally for [[Quest]] celebration and retrospection                                |
| **Facilitation**       | rotating Steward of the Week; decisions by consensus or consent                                                           |
| **Accountability**     | keep a living journal; one page per week; mutually witness individual and collective progress, impact, and transformation |
| **Autonomy**           | self-governing; may federate with other Circles into Guilds or Local Commons                                              |
| **Safety & Integrity** | peer support, transparency, immediate care protocols for emotional or energetic overwhelm                                 |

___

## How Circles Multiply

This is the heartbeat of [[The Great Game of LIONSBERG|The Great Game]].

Every person in a Circle is also part of **a second Circle** — the one they form with the 2 or 3 others they invite. This is the **double helix** through which the DNA of the [[New Civilization]] replicates.

The pattern is simple:

1. **Receive the invitation** from someone you know and trust
2. **Join or form a Circle** — gather 3 to 12 resonant souls
3. **Choose a [[Quest]]** — a tangible act of goodwill that transforms your community
4. **Do the work** — meet weekly, track commitments, learn, grow
5. **Pass [[The Flame]]** — each person invites 2 or 3 others who are ready

Those who are invited do not need to be convinced. They need to be *found*. They are already awake, already sensing the urgency, already seeking others who understand.

Within a week, each accepts the invitation and passes the Flame forward. The 2 or 3 become 5 become 13 become 144 become 250 million — not through campaigns or coordination, but through the ancient organic mathematics of trust and resonance.

This is how [[The First Three Percent]] emerges — approximately **[[The 300]]** per community of 10,000 — the empowered local leaders and teams who form the planetary grid of the New Civilization.

___

## What Makes a Circle Alive

A Circle is alive when its members:

- **Show up** — consistently, vulnerably, ready to work
- **Do real work** — a [[Quest]] that transforms, not just discussions
- **Hold each other** — in truth, in love, in accountability
- **Grow** — through reflection, learning, and deepening trust
- **Multiply** — by passing the Flame and helping new Circles form

A Circle that only meets but does not act is a study group. A Circle that acts but does not reflect is a task force. A Circle that does both — and multiplies — is a living cell of Heaven On Earth.
