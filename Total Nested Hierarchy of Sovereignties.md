# Total Nested Hierarchy of Sovereignties

_The architecture of freedom — sovereignty protected at every scale from the individual to the planetary._

___

## The Architecture

The LIONSBERG system is structured as a **total nested hierarchy of sovereignties** — each level a complete, self-governing whole, voluntarily integrated into larger wholes, all in service of [[ONE]] and [[All]].

Sovereign individuals...
...voluntarily associate into sovereign families...
...voluntarily associate into sovereign [[Circles]]...
...voluntarily associate into sovereign communities...
...voluntarily associate into sovereign bioregions...
...voluntarily associate into the planetary Whole...
...in service of [[ONE]] and [[All]].

___

## The Principles

**1. Sovereignty is retained at every level.** No higher-order form of organization absorbs the sovereignty of any lower-order form. The Circle does not absorb the individual. The community does not absorb the Circle. The federation does not absorb the community.

**2. Participation is voluntary at every level.** No one is compelled to join. No one is penalized for leaving. The system is held together by [[Love]], shared Purpose, and [[Influence]] — never by force.

**3. Authority flows from consent.** Power is delegated upward by those who are governed, never imposed downward by those who govern. This is the inversion of the [[Old World]] pattern, where power flows from the top and obedience is demanded from below.

**4. Each level serves the levels below it.** The federation serves the communities. The communities serve the Circles. The Circles serve the individuals. Service flows downward. Authority flows upward.

**5. The Pattern repeats fractally.** The same governance DNA — [[Democratic Self-Governance Through Shared Agreements and Wise Eldership]] — operates at every scale. What works for a Circle of 8 works for a community of 10,000 works for a bioregion of millions.

___

## Historical Precedent

**The Swiss Confederation**: 735 years of radical cantonal sovereignty within federal unity. Each canton has its own constitution, parliament, government, courts, and police. Four languages, multiple religions, unified by process rather than homogeneity.

**The Iroquois Confederacy**: Each nation maintained full internal sovereignty. The Confederacy addressed only matters of common concern. Unanimous consent required for major decisions.

**[[Mondragon]]**: Arm's-length federation. Each cooperative legally independent. The Core Enterprise of ~60 people serves 268 organizations. Individual cooperatives can fail without taking down the whole.

___

## The Flow of Power

In the [[Old World]], power accumulates at intermediate levels — in states, corporations, institutions — and is used to control those below.

In the [[New World]], power flows from [[ONE]], through the individual, upward through increasingly higher orders of voluntary association, and returns to [[ONE]].

**Any accumulation of power at an intermediate level is a sign of corruption that must be corrected.**

In the Old World, individuals were convicted for treason against the state.
In the New World, states are convicted for treason against the individual and the ONE.
