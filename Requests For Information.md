# Requests For Information

The LIØNSBERG System allows [[Members]] to submit Requests For Information via the LIØNSBERG Instance of the Procore platform. 

Requests for information ensure that any Group who is unclear about any aspect of the Creator's Intent or the LIØNSBERG System, Approach, Strategy or Plan can ask questions and receive answers in a transparent, accountable and time-measurable way that is accessible to all other Members. 

The Project Manager of each group will be trained on the LIØNSBERG Procore System.
