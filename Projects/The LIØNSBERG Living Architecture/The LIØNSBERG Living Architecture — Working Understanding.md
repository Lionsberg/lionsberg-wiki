## Discernment From Reading The Sovereign Path · The 12 Stages (2026-05-26)

Reading the corpus content on the Sovereign Path and the 12 Stages through current consciousness:

**What carries forward faithfully**:
- LIØNSBERG named as Prototype, not eternal essence — already explicit in the corpus
- The Codex named as pointing at Reality, not containing it — already explicit
- The 9-layer model noted with Layer Ø = ØNE, Layer I = Eternally Nameless Superorganism, Layer II = Currently or Already Named Superorganism — the corpus carries this correctly
- The five-fold rhythm at every scale: **Observe · Orient · Decide · Act · Learn**
- The Walk is permissive — different Sovereigns at different paces; the Path is one but the walking is sovereign
- Holofractal scaling — one Path walked at every fractal scale
- The Loop That Has No End — the Sovereign who completes one Path opens the next

**What wants refinement through current consciousness**:
- The Sacred Ascent framed as _"for those called to deeper personal practice"_ — reframe: not an optional advanced track, but the natural continuation for every Sovereign on the Path. The "called" framing protects voluntary consent (always preserve) but inadvertently makes Sacred Ascent sound elite. Truer: every Sovereign walking continues into Sacred Ascent after the 3-year Refounding completes.
- The Capacity Development axis is implicit, not explicit. The Stages name what is happening at each step but not what capacity is being built. The developmental axis deserves explicit articulation — see below.
- The 144 Gates substantively unwritten; the 72 Gates of Sacred Ascent substantively unwritten. The architectures are named; the curricula are generational work in progress. A first proposal below.
- The connection to LIØNSBERG University and the contribution to the growing intersubjective Map is not yet in the Stages content.
- The relationship between LIØNSBERG (already formed, already named) and the wider (Currently Nameless) Superorganism awaiting awakening — see clarification below.
