**Coherence observations:**

1. **Three temporal strata visible in this range.** (a) The 2022-23 generic handbook drafts (Roman-numeral series, value stubs, IV. cluster) — pre-Pattern-Language, lacking voice and frame. (b) The 2024 covenant/middle-period material (`In A Lionsberg World`, `J's Guide`, `Jordan and Faith`, `Jordan on Governance`, `Inbreaking and Displacing`) — strong content, but still pre-Caves and pre-Pattern-Language vocabulary. (c) The 2026 Pattern-Language-aware material (`Golden Seed`, `Holofractal Omnifederation`, `Govern Your Circles`, `Grow Your Circles`, `Integrating and Submitting Your Work`, `Integrated Delivery`, `How These Wikis Are Built`, [[J]], [[Help! I Am Lost]]). The 2026 layer is dramatically tighter and Codex-ready; the 2022 layer mostly is not.

2. **The phrase "Lionsberg System" appears 75+ times in this range.** The migration to "LIØNSBERG" (with Ø glyph) per Phase 1.5 has not propagated into most of these files. A global glyph-pass is owed across this entire alphabetical band.

3. **Source-elevation language to watch:** [[Jordan and Faith]] is deeply personal and beautiful but uses "the most pivotal figures in history" with Jesus as canonical example, calls Lionsberg's mission "ONE with the Mission of Christ," and centers Judeo-Christian framing. Per voice discipline, this material is appropriate as Jordan's personal testimony in Wiki Blogs, but Cards extracted from it must universalize. The Golden Thread (universal wisdom across traditions) is the safe form; Christ-as-canonical-archetype is not.

4. **Species-as-categorical-enemy risk:** [[Invisible Power Structures]] includes "extraterrestrial, interdimensional, or other unseen entities" within the adversary frame. Per `Cosmic Wildlife` discipline, this needs surgical reframing: the enemy is *kontrolle and parasitism wherever they manifest*, not species. The Card on Visible/Invisible Power Structures is highly valuable, but the categorical alignment of "interdimensional beings = adversary" must be revised to "parasitic patterns of consciousness, wherever embodied."

5. **The 115-line [[In A Lionsberg World]] is a covenant-corpus.** It is too dense to function as a single Card but too rich to dissolve. Recommendation: convert to a *seed-field* — read each line as a candidate Card, and harvest the strongest 20-30 (e.g., #34 "Co-Create The Conditions for Flourishing and Abundant Life," #37 "Leave Each Domain A Little Better Than We Found It," #61 "Through the Eye of the Needle voluntarily," #74 "Everything Is A Project," #87 "Resurrect and Revivify the Plan without deviating from Values or The Goal," #95 "We eternally remember that We Do Not Know," #105 "Drive Marginal Cost Toward Zero").
