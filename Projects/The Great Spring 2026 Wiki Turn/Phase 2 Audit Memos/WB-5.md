## 8. Coherence Observations

1. **The cluster is over-built.** 12 books is too many. The core argument fits in 1 unified volume. The redundancy reflects the iterative way the material was discovered, not the structure it should ultimately take.

2. **MPHNI is the canonical Voice.** It is markedly more disciplined, less species-essentialist, less reactive, more prophetic-yet-precise than the other books. It points the way for what the entire cluster's Voice should become. If only one book in this cluster survived, MPHNI would be sufficient.

3. **The Great UnVeiling is the canonical structure.** Its 12-Gate cathedral architecture is theologically richer than MPHNI's 11-part diagnosis-to-action arc. The combined work would use GU's structure with MPHNI's voice.

4. **AFG, ET, Beyond Earth are early-stage drafts.** They are foundational, but the Voice has matured significantly since. They preserve testimony that should not be lost, but they are not the form the work takes going forward.

5. **The 66 Most Protected Narratives is the master research register.** Only 4 of 66 are written. This is the right scaffold for ongoing investigation. Each of the 66 narrative-Cards corresponds to a Card-set in the Codex.

6. **The 99 Theses is the canonical short form.** It exists. It is dense, polished enough to nail to the gates. It should be treated as a primary artifact and lightly translated where Voice-discipline requires.

7. **The cluster's relationship to the rest of the Codex:** The Cosmic/Disclosure/Crisis Cluster is the **diagnostic and prophetic-disclosure pole**. Its counterpart is the LIØNSBERG Way / Playbook / Pattern Language / 12 Pillars — the **constructive-pattern pole**. Both poles are necessary. The diagnostic pole describes what is collapsing and why; the constructive pole describes what is being co-created and how. The cluster must end at the bridge into the constructive material, as MPHNI 11.3 explicitly does.
