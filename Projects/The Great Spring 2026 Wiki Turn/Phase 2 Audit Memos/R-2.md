**Coherence observations:**

1. **The Ca-Co semantic neighborhood is the wiki's densest concept cluster.** The range alphabetically covered here is roughly the Lexicon's most foundational tier: Circle, Commons, Community, Commitment, Communication, Co-Creation, Co-Operation, Collapse, Collaboration. If the Volumes need a "civic-architecture concentrate," it lives here.

2. **The wiki's Pirsig-card maturity is bimodal.** Some pages (`Choose A Quest`, `Choose Your Roles`, `Connect and Collaborate With Other Circles`, `Continuously Improve Everything`, `Co-Create LIØNSBERG Learning Institutions`, `Circle`, `Central Animating Spirit`, `Capital Subordinate To Purpose`) are fully voiced, sacred-and-practical, ready for the Sacred Codex with minor edits. Others (`Better`, `Bottom Up`, `Compassion`, `Cleanliness`, `Conscientiousness`) are single-paragraph stubs that are technically correct but devoid of voice. The Phase-3 fold should not flatten this bimodality — small Cards and large Cards both belong.

3. **Three Cardable concepts hide inside "Capital Subordinate To Purpose"** as one document: (a) the architectural principle "capital serves, never governs," (b) Graham Boyd's ownership-as-deepest-attractor insight, (c) the Mondragon evidence-stack, (d) the Lionsberg implementation (Time Currency, LUV, Voluntary Tithing, Fractal Commonsing). Each is fold-worthy alone. The current page is dense in the best sense and could spawn 3-4 sibling Cards.

4. **The Disclosure / Cosmic Wildlife thread needs editorial discipline.** `Bob Dean`, `Bill Uhouse`, `Clifford Stone`, `Daniel Sheehan`, `David Grusch`, `CIA and NATO Insights…`, `Caution On the Powerful Creatures from Above`, `Controlled Disclosure Marches Towards Its Peak` constitute a major thread in the wiki, and several of these pages **flirt with source-elevation and species-as-enemy framing** that the [[Cosmic Wildlife]] principle explicitly forbids. Specifically: `Caution On the Powerful Creatures from Above` contains language treating "the powerful ones" as a species essence (Elohim) rather than as factions. This is a coherence-edit opportunity for Phase 2.8 — re-voice as "factions of cosmic wildlife including humans" rather than "those creatures vs us."

5. **The Citizen-Led cluster is articulated extremely well but scattered.** `Citizen Led`, `Citizen Led Self-Governance`, `Citizen Led Response`, `Citizen Led Public Private Partnership`, `Citizens`, `Citizens of Goodwill` form a five-card constellation that needs a single index/parent page. The pieces are excellent; the navigation is implicit.

6. **The Pattern Language has a hidden meta-structure here.** Each major Element of the 12 Elements seems to have generated a cluster of Cards in the Ca-Co range:
   - Element 1 (Shared Vision/Purpose): `Creator's Intent`, `Central Animating Spirit`, `Creator`
   - Element 2 (Holofractal Omnifederation): `Circle`, `Circles`, `Circle of Circles and Team of Teams`, `Community of Communities`, `Communities`
   - Element 3 (Field of Agreements): `Consent`, `Covenant Relationship`, `Constitution`
   - Element 4 (Governance): `Citizen Led Self-Governance`, `Conditions of Satisfaction For System Of Governance`, `Constitutional Amendments`, `Commander's Intent`, `Campaigning and Making Promises`
   - Element 5 (Capital Subordinate): `Capital Subordinate To Purpose`, `Commons`, `Commonwealth`, `Cooperation Vs. Competition`, `Core Enterprise`
   - Element 6 (Education): `Co-Create LIØNSBERG Learning Institutions`, `Capacity Building`, `Catalytic Resources`, `Current Best Understanding`, `Continuous Learning And Improvement`
   - Element 7 (Communication/Culture): `Communication`, `Collective Notetaking`, `Collaboration`, `Bottom Line Up Front`
   - Element 8 (Structural Immunity): `Concretizing The Absolute` (idolatry as failure mode), `Cultivating Healthy Interdependencies`
   - Element 11 (Network of Commitments): `Commitment Based Management`, `Commitments`, `Blocked Time`
   - Element 12 (Continuous Improvement): `Continuous Improvement`, `Continuous Learning And Improvement`, `Continuously Improve Everything`, `Cultivating The Green Shoots`
   
   This implicit structure is real and should be honored when Phase 3 folds.

7. **"Conditions" is a buried Card-meta-term.** It appears in: `Conditions of Satisfaction`, `Conditions of Satisfaction For System Of Governance`, `Total Nested Hierarchy of Conditions`, `Cultivating The Green Shoots` (different conditions for each Stage of Development), `Covenant Relationship` (conditions for the relationship). This is one of those LIØNSBERG cross-cutting verbs that probably deserves a meta-Card.

8. **Case Studies operate at the right Pirsig grain for "proof-of-concept" cards.** The five Case Studies in this range (`Kiva Pilgrimage`, `Palouse`, `Precision Measurement / Living Systems Data`, `Soft Franchises / Business In A Box`, `Transcription and Translation`) demonstrate the system across very different scales — global indigenous pilgrimage, regional food-system transformation, planetary data infrastructure, intrapreneurial federation, micro-workflow problem-solving. They serve as evidence-cards. For the Sacred Codex, they may belong as appendices to the relevant Volumes rather than as primary Cards.

9. **The voice is uneven across this range.** Some pages voice the LIØNSBERG register cleanly (`Choose A Quest`, `Central Animating Spirit`, `Continuously Improve Everything`). Others retain the older "the [[Receiver]] recognized…" or "In the context of the LIØNSBERG System…" patterns that read as scaffolding rather than as final voice. Phase 2.8 voice-pass needed.

10. **Many cards over-link.** Some Cards like `Cultivating Healthy Interdependencies` have wiki links on most concept-words. This is correct for hypertext but visually noisy on a printed page. The Sacred Codex fold may want a "link-density" style guide — Cards in the bound volume probably show ~5-15 hot-links, not 30+.
