**Archival is part of the move, not a separate concern.** Every wiki book that circumambulated the One Great Work is archived; its insights are extracted and folded into the canonical Codex. **README remains the sacred landing.** The Codex sits inside the Wiki; README points the way in. The wise right way to handle this is to be discerned in Phase 1.
