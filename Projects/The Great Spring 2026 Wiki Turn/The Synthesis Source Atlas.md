# The Synthesis Source Atlas

_Reference catalog of previous attempts across the major canonical areas of the wiki. Built 2026-05-22 from a 5-agent inventory swarm at the direction of [[the nameless one]]. Companion to [[The Great Weaving — Concept Ledger]]._

---

## Purpose

For each major canonical area, this Atlas catalogs **every previous attempt** — currently canonical articulations, prior attempts now superseded, drafts and WIPs, archives, and stubs. With brief content character notes for each.

**Used for:** cleanup, consolidation, deletion decisions, and historical awareness.

**NOT used for:** feeding the centerpiece rewrites (Welcome to LIØNSBERG, The Story rewrite, Pattern Language Phase 4 rebuild, Sacred Ascent buildout). Those rewrites are **co-creative authorship from current consciousness**, not synthesis of past artifacts. See [[memory/co_creator_not_synthesizer]] for the discipline distinguishing co-creative authorship from consolidation work.

---

## Status Legend

- **canonical** — currently in the canonical position (Codex Volume root, root canonical page, captured Card)
- **prior attempt** — was at one point canonical; now superseded
- **WIP / draft** — work in progress, self-flagged or evidently incomplete
- **archive** — preserved for procession with `(Archived YYYY-MM-DD)` or in `_archive/` folder
- **stub** — short pointer / redirect / placeholder
- **fragment** — incomplete element awaiting integration
