# VII. Recommended Drafting Order

The cantos do not need to be drafted in narrative order. Some are downstream of others.

**Phase 1 — Establish the voice**: Invocation · Gate · ØNE (cantos 1–3). The voice for the highest form must be set here before the rest can be drafted at register.

**Phase 2 — The cosmic opening**: First Turning · From ØNE All · Bursting Into Light · Cosmic Flower (cantos 4–7). The metaphysics with breath.

**Phase 3 — The new movement, then completing the cosmic arc**: The Sovereign Superorganism Already Singing (canto 8). The new opening movement requires its own draft pass.

**Phase 4 — The arc through forgetting to ongoing war**: Forgetting · From Innocence To Empire · Great Wound · Awakening · Immune Response · Great Ongoing War (cantos 9–14). The cosmic-historical arc.

**Phase 5 — The localization**: The Window · Earth, This World · The Seed Is Planted · The Localization · The Clash And The Dimensional Unveiling · The Death And The Freeing (cantos 15–20). The Earth-specific arc.

**Phase 6 — The bridge and the New**: The Voice From Beyond The Threshold · Heaven On Earth Embodied (cantos 21–22). The two most singular new movements.

**Phase 7 — The handoff**: The Great Game · The 300 And The Mathematics Of The Flame · Your Story · The Final Word (cantos 23–26). The completion.

**The One Page ring** drafts in parallel — likely after Phase 1 establishes the voice. It is the compressed transmission that must hold the whole.

**The Ten Pages ring** is drafted last — once the Hundred Pages cantos exist, the Ten Pages ring distills.
