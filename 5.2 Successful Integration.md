# 5.2 Successful Integration

We will know [[The Sacred Flame]] has been successfully passed when each new co-creator: 

1. Accepts [[The Invitation]]   
2. Forges or Joins at least one Circle  
3. Embarks on a [[Seasonal Quests|Seasonal Quest]]  
4. Shares [[Stories of Transformation]]  
5. [[Pass The Flame|Passes The Flame]] to the next Wise Right person
