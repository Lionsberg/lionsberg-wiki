# 2.2 The Three Supreme Agreements

Before there are plans or projects,
Before there are Circles or Quests,
Before any structure rises —
There must be a foundation that cannot be shaken.

That foundation is **The Three Supreme Agreements**.

---

## The Agreements

Life beyond the threshold revolves around voluntary commitments — freely chosen, faithfully kept. Among all possible agreements, three stand supreme. They are the root from which every value, every practice, and every form of governance in [[The Great Game of LIONSBERG|The Great Game]] ultimately grows:

1. **Love [[ONE]].**
2. **Love One Another.**
3. **Love All.**

That is it. Three agreements. Simple enough for a child to understand. Deep enough to organize a civilization.

---

## What They Mean

**Love ONE** — Orient your life toward the Ultimate. Toward the Source and Sustainer of all that is Good, True, and Beautiful. This is not a theological abstraction. It is a lived commitment to align your thoughts, words, and actions with the Highest you can perceive. It is the decision to serve what is eternal rather than what is expedient.

**Love One Another** — Treat every fellow traveler as sacred. Not as a resource, not as a rival, not as a means to an end — but as a sovereign expression of the same Spirit that animates you. This is the basis of every [[Circle]], every covenant, every act of mutual aid. It means showing up with honesty, generosity, and care. It means holding one another accountable in truth and grace.

**Love All** — Extend that same sacred regard to all of Creation. To those you will never meet. To future generations. To the land, the waters, the creatures, the living systems that sustain us. This is the agreement that prevents any community from becoming a tribe turned inward, hoarding goodness for its own. Love All keeps the circle open. Always.

---

## Why These Three

From these three agreements, the entire system of values naturally unfolds. Justice, mercy, courage, humility, stewardship, truthfulness — all of them are expressions of Love rightly directed toward ONE, toward One Another, and toward All.

You do not need a thousand rules when you have three agreements this deep. They carry within them a kind of spiritual intelligence — an intuitive clarity about which patterns of action lead toward flourishing and which lead toward destruction.

When a decision is difficult, return here.
When a conflict arises, return here.
When the path ahead is unclear, return here.

*Does this choice reflect love of ONE? Does it honor one another? Does it serve All?*

If the answer is yes, move forward with confidence.
If not, pause and discern again.

---

## The Backbone of the Covenant

The Three Supreme Agreements are not suggestions. They are the backbone of the **Covenant** — the living [[Field of Agreements]] that protects every participant in The Great Game.

They ensure that the Game can be played across all cultures, all communities, all generations — not through rigid control, but through a shared orientation so deep it becomes second nature.

They are what make it possible for strangers to become allies overnight. For Circles to trust one another across oceans. For a planetary movement to cohere without centralized command.

**Love ONE. Love One Another. Love All.**

Memorize them. Embody them. Let them become the quiet law written on your heart.

Everything else in this Playbook flows from here.
