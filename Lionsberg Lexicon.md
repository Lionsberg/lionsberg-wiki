## The System 

1. In response to [[Meta Crisis]] and [[Meta Opportunity]], [[We]] have [[Work]] to do.  
2. We call the [[Total Nested Hierarchy of Work]] required to accomplish the [[Total Nested Hierarchy of Goals]] the [[Meta Project]].  
3. [[Work]] requires the transfer of [[Energy]] via the application of [[Force]] along a [[Vector]] towards an [[Aim]].  
4. We call the [[Vector]] the [[Critical Path]] towards [[The Goal]].  
5. [[Work]] at scale demands a [[System]].   
6. A [[System]] demands a [[Goal]], in this case, [[The Goal]] / [[Meta Goal]]. 
7. The [[Meta Project]] demands a [[Meta System]].  
8. We call the [[Incarnation]] of this the [[Lionsberg System]].  
9. [[Theory Of Constraints]] / [[Lean]] / [[Waste]] / [[Value]] / [[Value Stream Mapping]]  
10. The [[Lionsberg System]] is designed as an Interoperable [[System of Systems]]  
11. It is designed to be a [[New Human Operating System]], and it includes a [[Lionsberg Human System Living System Interface]].  
12. [[The System]] is what enables [[The Whole]] to co-operate as [[One Body]].
