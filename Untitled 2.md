# Untitled 2

Fellow Travelers,  

Over the last several years, we have been increasingly coming to consensus that many of us are all here for "this" and working towards the same ultimate project, future and goal.  

We have collectively convened a variety of working groups and explorations amounting to tens of thousands of hours of calls, notes, transcripts, etc. 

We have networked everyone required for "this" to within one degree. 

Now, the predicted shaking and phase shift of Earth is clearly accelerating. 

It appears that the elders, strategists, and planners might have been correct when they converged on the stark assessment that we have only until December 2026 to connect, empower, and align the first few percent of the population. 

The good news is that through years of hard work we have arrived at broad consensus of the essential, ancient, and universal patterns that an emerging regenerative social superorganism sub-composed of "us" would need to embody. We have also solved the key technological challenges to a level that the pattern can be enabled to interoperate and actually "work" at planetary scale. 

We have also come to universally understand that each of us has precious gifts and puzzle pieces to bring to the table, and that none of us can do "this" alone. 

We have reached the end of what can be figured out intellectually. 
The rest of the Path lies in transformative embodied learning, action, and interbecoming.  

There is no center, foundation, or top to "this" but the ONE Absolute and Ultimately Ineffable principle among All.  

Whatever we do together belongs to no one except its sovereign co-creators. 

My purpose is writing this is to ensure that no one "we" have been collaborating is involuntarily left behind and we begin next steps to co-create and become the New Civilization.
