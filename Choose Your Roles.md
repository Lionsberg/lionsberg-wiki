# Choose Your Roles

From before the foundations of the world,
There has always been a place only you could fill.
Not a title. Not a mask. Not a job description.
But the truest expression of **who you really are** —
A living note in the Symphony of [[ONE]].

Now, gathered in your [[Circle]], with a [[Quest]] before you,
The question ripens:

**What is the part only you can play?**

---

## Role Is Not Assigned — It Is Discovered

In the Old World, roles are handed down from above. Someone in authority decides what you are and where you fit. You wear the mask. You perform the function. You forget who you really are.

In the [[New Civilization]], it works the other way.

Your role emerges from the intersection of:

- **The gifts you carry** — what you are naturally drawn to and unusually good at
- **The longings written on your soul** — what sets your heart on fire
- **The experiences that have prepared you** — even the painful ones, especially the painful ones
- **The cry of the hour** — what your Circle and community need right now

When these converge, your role is not chosen. It is **recognized**. Often others will see it in you before you see it in yourself. Your Circle becomes a mirror, revealing gifts you struggle to name on your own.

---

## The Archetypal Constellations

Across civilizations and ages, certain patterns of calling appear like stars in the firmament. These are not costumes to put on — they are **currents of being** you will recognize when they rise and flow through you:

- **Keepers** — carriers of Spirit, bearers of the Sacred Fire, tenders of memory and meaning
- **Pathfinders** — trail-breakers who cut a way through the unknown and report back
- **Guardians** — protectors of what is sacred, defenders of the vulnerable
- **Weavers** — gatherers of many strands into one living tapestry of relationship and coherence
- **Voices** — truth-speakers who name what others sense but cannot articulate
- **Healers** — menders of the torn, restorers of the broken, tenders of body and soul
- **Builders** — makers of tools, structures, and systems that serve the New World
- **Visionaries** — seers of what is not yet, who call it forth into being
- **Integrators** — harmonizers who turn fragments into wholeness and vision into reality

Your Circle does not need one of each. Some roles will overlap. Some will remain latent until the right Quest calls them forth. The constellation shifts as the Circle matures and new challenges arise.

**The point is not to label yourself. The point is to listen — to what rises in you, to what your Circle reflects back, to what the work itself reveals.**

---

## The Two Coordinators

Every Circle elects **two coordinators** — not bosses, not managers, but servant-leaders who keep the Circle functioning:

- Ensuring meetings happen on rhythm
- Keeping communications flowing between gatherings
- Tracking commitments and gently holding accountability
- Making sure no one falls through the cracks

Two, not one — so the weight is shared, so neither carries it alone, so the function of stewardship has its own witness and balance.

These roles should **rotate** as the Circle matures. Leadership in the New Civilization is not a permanent position. It is a season of service that passes from hand to hand.

---

## How Roles Emerge in Practice

Do not try to assign all roles on your first day. Instead:

1. **Begin your Quest.** The work itself is the great revealer. Notice who naturally steps into what.
2. **Name what you see in each other.** After a few weeks, take time to reflect: *What gifts have shown up? What roles are each of us naturally filling?*
3. **Invite honest reflection.** Ask each member: *Where do I feel most alive in our work? Where do I feel I contribute most? Where do I feel stretched?*
4. **Adjust and evolve.** Roles are not permanent. As the Circle grows and new Quests arise, the constellation shifts. Someone who was a Builder in one season may become a Healer in the next.

The goal is not a perfect org chart. The goal is a living body where every member knows their gift is needed, their voice is heard, and their contribution matters.

As you discover who you are in the small, intimate context of your Circle,

Let the true pattern be revealed.

There is nothing the world needs more
Than people who know who they are
And are **coming fully alive**.
