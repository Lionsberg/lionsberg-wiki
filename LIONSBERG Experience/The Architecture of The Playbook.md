## An Evolving Co-Creation 

The LIONSBERG Playbook is designed to be co-created  
by **[[The First Three Percent]]** as they play and embody **The Great Game of LIONSBERG**.  

The lived experiences, opportunities, gifts, offers, and needs of the early players will be what makes the Playbook accurate, organic, and emergent  
for the rest of the population that follows in due course.   

What is provided to date is only a starting point for emergence and continuous improvement. 

## Quick Start Guide

**[[The LIONSBERG Quick Start Guide]]** provides an overview  
of the essentials required to quickly step into **The Great Game**.  

If you got to this point via **[[THE NAMELESS BOOK]]**, you have likely already read it.

Everything that follows can be accessed, adjusted, and co-created as the journey unfolds.
