# Seasonal OmniSpection

Every solstice and equinox — roughly every 90 days —
Every [[Circles|Circle]], Community, and Guild pauses.
Not to stop. To **see from above**.

This is the **Seasonal OmniSpection** — a sacred rhythm of review, celebration, and renewal that keeps the living Body honest, learning, and aligned with [[The Goal]].

---

## The Rhythm

The Seasonal OmniSpection follows the natural pulse of the earth — the turning of seasons that has governed human work and worship since the beginning.

At each turning, the rhythm is the same:

- **Complete your [[Quest]]** — Bring the season's work to its natural conclusion. Finish what can be finished. Name what remains.
- **Celebrate** — Honor what was accomplished. Not with false praise, but with genuine recognition of real work done by real people.
- **Acknowledge what transformed** — In the community. In your Circle. In yourselves. Name the changes — visible and invisible.
- **Retrospect** — What worked? What didn't? What surprised us? What did we learn? What would we do differently?
- **Share your learnings** — Offer your experience to other Circles and to the [[Commons]]. Your hard-won wisdom is a gift to the Whole.
- **Choose your next Quest** — Look ahead. What is the need? What is the call? What is the wisest next move for the coming season?
- **Set your commitments** — Clear, concrete, shared. What will we do? By when? How will we know?
- **[[Pass The Flame]]** — Who is ready to be invited? Who have you met this season who is already awake, already seeking? Extend the invitation.

---

## Why the Seasons

This rhythm is not arbitrary. It mirrors the natural cycles of planting, growing, harvesting, and resting that have shaped every civilization that endured.

A [[Quest]] is roughly one season long — long enough to accomplish something real, short enough to maintain urgency and focus. Ninety days is the natural unit of transformation.

The seasonal pause ensures the movement does not just grow — it **learns**. It adapts. It improves with every cycle. A Circle that has completed four Seasonal OmniSpections has a full year of lived wisdom. A movement of thousands of Circles, each contributing their seasonal learnings to the [[Commons]], accumulates intelligence at a pace no centralized institution could match.

Over time, this accumulated wisdom becomes the **living intelligence of the [[New Civilization]]** — not stored in a database, but embodied in the practices, stories, and relationships of the people who are building it.

---

## The Practice

The Seasonal OmniSpection can be as simple as a single gathering — two to three hours, perhaps shared with food and fellowship. Or it can unfold over several days, woven into the natural rhythms of a community celebration.

What matters is that it happens. Every Circle. Every season. Without fail.

**The Circle that reflects together, grows together.
The Circle that celebrates together, endures together.
The Circle that chooses its next Quest together, transforms together.**

This is how Heaven On Earth is built — not in one great leap, but season by season, Quest by Quest, Circle by Circle, each one wiser than the last.
