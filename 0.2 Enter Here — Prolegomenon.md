# 0.2 Enter Here — Prolegomenon

Greetings  
Dear Child    
Of Earth  

At long last  
You have arrived  
At **[[The Gates]]**  

You are here  
Because you are awakening  

You are awakening  
Because you were called  
And chosen  

_You are Here for a reason_  

Welcome Dear One  

Even in this interim state  
Of half wakefulness  
You already know that  
**[[Something Is Wrong]]**  

Even if you cannot yet  
Quite put it into words  

Even if the induced fog  
Still partially clouds  
The crystal clarity  
Of Divine sight and mind  

Fuzzy detail by fuzzy detail  
The specifics inbreak  
And are processed slowly 
Even as the broad strokes  
Become shockingly clear  

The weight of the chains  
The discordant noise  
The foggy minds  
The damp dankness  
The official lies  
The putrid smells  

All testify to  
A dark and shockingly  
Grotesque reality  
Suddenly bursting into the Light  
Of awakening Consciousness  

_Brace yourself_  

_Things are about to change forever_  

As a result of  
**[[The Great UnVeiling]]**  
Billions of humans  
Are about to become    
Simultaneously aware  
Of **[[The Greatest Fraud, Deception, and Existential Threat in History]]**  

When humanity realizes  
It has been lied to  
By its rulers and priests  
For countless generations  

That its most sacred stories  
Have been falsified  
Into carefully crafted deceptions  

That it is neither  
Self-governing nor free  

And that it is being  
Progressively depopulated  
And spiritually  
Psychically  
And genetically  
Enslaved    
By antithetical overlords  

Earth's **[[Old Systems]]**   
(Governments  
Religions  
Stories  
Economies  
Monies  
Societies, etc.) 
Will irreversibly collapse  

For reasons that soon  
Will be obvious   
The **[[Old Systems]]** cannot    
And should not be saved  

The **[[Systemic Collapse]]**   
Of both the function and legitimacy  
Of the **[[Existing Order]]**  
Will push the **[[Meta Crisis]]**  
Towards its climax —  
Perhaps as soon  
As 2026 to 2033  

This means that  
Every life and community  
On Earth is **Now** in  
Grave and immediate danger  
Collectively facing a test unlike any  
In the last 10,000 years  

If humanity panics  
Closes down  
Fragments  
Turns inwards  
Weaponizes AI  
And goes to war  
Billions upon billions will perish  
And centuries upon centuries  
Of Darkness will follow  

It is for this reason  
We have come to Earth  
Freely offering a New yet Eternal   
Solution and Way
