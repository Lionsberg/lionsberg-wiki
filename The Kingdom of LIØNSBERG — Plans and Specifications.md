## Navigating Towards Truth

_The disciplines by which the People of LIØNSBERG continually orient toward what IS — across all scales, all domains, all epistemic registers._

The Plans and Specifications cannot stand on assumed truth. The New Civilization is built on **navigated** truth — truth pursued, tested, revealed, refined. This section names the disciplines by which that navigation proceeds.

### Truth-Seeking Is Constitutional

Truth-seeking is not an option for the People; it is a constitutional commitment. No dogma is canonical. No received doctrine is exempt from inquiry. The Quest to Perceive, to Know, to ReMember, to Discern is perpetual — woven into every Circle, every OmniSpection, every season.

### The Spectrum of Knowing

Knowledge is not single. The People recognize multiple legitimate modes of knowing — each appropriate to its domain:

- **Empirical observation** — what is verifiable through the senses, instruments, and replicable measurement
- **Logical inference** — what follows from established premises by sound reasoning
- **Transmitted witness** — what is held by first-hand testimony, accountable to the witness, weighed by the witness's track record and integrity
- **Hypothesis** — what is proposed but not yet verified; held as provisional, treated as testable
- **Archetypal true myth** — what is described in archetypal language that maps actual reality (The Caves, The Wilderness, The Sacred Mountain)
- **Convention** — what is agreed by the community as canonical (defined terms, structural decisions, ratified Articles)
- **Direct gnosis** — what is known by direct communion with the [[ØNE]] in deep stillness, prayer, contemplation, embodied practice — accountable to the discernment of Circles and the resonance of the whole

No single mode is sovereign. Each domain calls for its appropriate epistemic register. To insist that empirical-method alone is valid is to truncate Reality; to elevate gnosis without empirical accountability is to drift from the world that IS.

### The Hierarchy of Epistemic Standards

When integrating multiple modes, the People honor a working hierarchy:

1. **Empirical evidence** — privileged for empirical claims
2. **Logical inference** — privileged when premises are well-established
3. **Hypothetical modeling** — held provisionally; clearly labeled as such
4. **Symbolic / archetypal narrative** — read as true myth, not confused with empirical claim
5. **Metaphorical framing** — read as pointing, not as literal mapping

Claims are explicitly labeled by mode. Speculation is not collapsed into certainty. Confidence levels are surfaced and stated.

### The Verification Filters

Every substantive claim that aspires to canonical status passes through five filters:

1. **Coherence with the [[ØNE]]** — does this move toward wholeness, integration, [[Right Relationship]] — or toward fragmentation, separation, domination?
2. **Coherence with the Pattern Language** — does this resonate with the canonical design grammar, or fight it?
3. **Suppression analysis** — has this knowledge been suppressed by old power-structures, and by whom, and why? Suppression often signals the territory of important Truth.
4. **Cross-domain integration** — does this resonate across multiple independent domains (science, contemplative tradition, lived practice, multiple cultures)?
5. **Harmonic resonance** — does this vibrate in tune with Reality as the People experience it in Circles, OmniSpection, embodied practice?

A claim that passes all five filters earns canonical weight. A claim that fails one or more is held as hypothesis, refined, retested.

### Epistemic Humility

All canonical articulation is provisional. The articulation today is not the articulation in five years. The Pattern itself does not change — but the words used to render the Pattern continually sharpen.

This humility extends even to the Eternal Provisions: those are recognized as eternal not because the People claim infallibility in articulating them, but because what they point to is itself the eternal ground. The pointing-words sharpen across generations; what they point to does not.

### The Refusal of Dogma

Truth-seeking is constitutional. Received doctrine is not. Any articulation may be revisited. Any Article may be amended (within the bounds of Article XII.3 of [[The LIØNSBERG Constitution]] — including the Eternal Provisions of Article XII.1). The Constitution is itself subject to OmniSpection.

### The Right of New Hypothesis

Sovereign beings retain the constitutional right to propose new hypotheses — including hypotheses that contradict received articulations — without penalty, without recrimination, without social cost beyond the natural cost of being wrong when one is. Persecution of hypothesis is itself constitutionally forbidden.

### Continuous OmniSpection

Truth is tested by [[OmniSpection]] — the 360-degree review, conducted at seasonal cadence (solstice and equinox at minimum), in which Circles examine: what is actually happening, what the canonical articulation says is happening, where drift has occurred, what is being learned. OmniSpection is how the corpus stays accountable to Reality.

### The Cosmic Knowledge Repository

The full corpus of LIØNSBERG functions as something like an error-correcting code for the cosmic knowledge field — sufficiently coherent, sufficiently redundant, sufficiently cross-verified that local errors can be detected and corrected by resonance with the whole. The Pattern Language, the Lexicon, the Constitution, the Cards, the Volumes — together they form a living architecture against which any local claim can be tested.
