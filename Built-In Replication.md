_The seventh irreducible element of [[The Core Pattern]]._

## The Principle

In every successful self-replicating movement, **the mechanism of spread is not separate from the practice — it IS the practice.**

The final step of maturity IS planting new seeds. Recovery requires transmission. Discipleship requires multiplication. The fruit of the tree contains new seeds.

**The act of passing the pattern forward is integral to the pattern itself.**
