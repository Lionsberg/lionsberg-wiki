# New Civilization

_The living alternative that emerges as the [[Old World]] collapses — not a revolution against the Old, but a conscious co-creation of the New._

___

## What The New Civilization Is

The New Civilization is not a government. It is not an institution. It is not a program.

**The New Civilization is the total set of properly functioning, rightly related communities, economies, and governance structures** operating according to [[The Core Pattern]] — the [[Twelve Irreducible Elements]] proven across 1,500 years of human history.

It is built from the ground up through:
- **[[Circles]]** of 5-16 people as the basic unit
- **Communities** of federated Circles addressing real needs at local scale
- **Bioregional Networks** coordinating food systems, education, healthcare, governance
- **The Planetary Grid** — **[[The 300]]** in every community of 10,000, connected globally

___

## How It Emerges

The New Civilization does not require permission from existing institutions. It does not depend on governments, corporations, or any existing power structure. It grows from the ground up, organically, through the voluntary association of sovereign beings awakening to their shared Purpose.

**By the time the existing structures fully collapse, the New Civilization is already functional.**

Not as a protest. Not as a revolution. As a living alternative — already governing, already feeding, already educating, already healing, already flourishing.
