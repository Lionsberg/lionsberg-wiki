# Archive

_The Procession. Preserved with provenance._

---

## What This Is

The Archive holds wiki content that is no longer canonical but is preserved for its historical and procession value. It is the canonical home for material that has been:

- **Folded** into a canonical Volume of [[The LIØNSBERG Sacred Codex]], with its substance now living in canonical form elsewhere
- **Superseded** by a more mature articulation
- **Retired** from active canon (where its content is fully metabolized into the Codex or the [[The LIØNSBERG Living Library|Living Library]])

The Archive is not the trash. It is the **record of the procession** — the work that brought the present canonical body of work into being, honored as part of the lineage rather than erased.

---

## Discipline

When material moves into the Archive:

1. **Verify the canonical destination** — the content's essential substance must live somewhere in the canonical body (a Codex Volume, a Living Library entry, or canonical Cards). Without that, archival is loss, not preservation.
2. **Preserve provenance** — each archived item retains a brief note of when it was archived, what superseded it, and where its substance now lives.
3. **Move rather than delete** — the file is moved here, not deleted, so the git history and the wiki itself preserve the lineage.
4. **Update inbound references** — wiki links that previously pointed to the archived item should be updated to point to its canonical destination, with the archived version reachable as the procession reference.

Each archived item is held under a brief disposition note explaining what was archived, when, and why.

## What Does NOT Go Here

- **Currently canonical material** — anything actively part of [[The LIØNSBERG Sacred Codex|the Codex]], the [[The LIØNSBERG Living Library|Living Library]], or active Card-extraction. That material lives in its canonical location.
- **Material not yet processed** — wiki books and pages whose disposition has not yet been discerned remain in their current location until Phase 3.B.2 processes them.
- **Working drafts in active development** — drafts under active refinement remain in their canonical location, marked as Work In Progress.
- **The wider wiki body of writing** — Above The Chaos essays, books in progress, the broader corpus — these remain in their wiki book folders unless and until a deliberate archive decision is made.
