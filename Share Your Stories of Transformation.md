# Share Your Stories of Transformation

The Pattern does not spread through propaganda.
It does not spread through arguments, campaigns, or clever marketing.
It spreads the way fire has always spread —
Through the undeniable evidence of **something real that happened**.

A life changed. A neighborhood healed. A [[Circles|Circle]] of strangers became a family. A [[Quest]] that seemed impossible was accomplished — and everyone who touched it was transformed in the process.

**That is a story worth telling.**

---

## Why Stories Matter

Stories are the oldest technology of transmission. Long before doctrine, before scripture, before systems — there were stories told around fires by people whose lives had been marked by the Real.

In [[The Great Game of LIONSBERG|The Great Game]], stories of transformation serve a sacred function:

- They are **living proof** that the Pattern works
- They are how those still in the Caves begin to see light
- They are how the sleeping begin to stir
- They are how trust is built between strangers who sense the same call

The best invitation is never an argument. It is a story of something real that happened when a few people chose to gather, choose a [[Quest]], and do the work.

---

## How to Tell a Good Story

When your Circle completes a Quest, when transformation happens, when something real shifts — write it down. Share it with other Circles. Offer it to the [[Commons]].

A good story of transformation answers five questions:

- **What was the need?** — What brokenness, gap, or longing did you see?
- **What did you do?** — What Quest did your Circle undertake? How did you work together?
- **What changed?** — In the community. In your Circle. In yourselves.
- **What did you learn?** — What surprised you? What was harder or easier than expected?
- **What would you do differently?** — What wisdom can you pass to the next Circle?

Keep it honest. Keep it specific. The power is not in polish — it is in truth.

---

## The Chain of Resonance

Stories create **resonance**.
Resonance creates **trust**.
Trust creates **invitation**.
Invitation creates **Circles**.
Circles create [[Heaven On Earth]].

This is the chain. Every story you tell is a link in it.

When someone hears what your Circle accomplished — not in theory but in lived reality — something stirs in them. They think: *I could do that. I know people who would gather for that. We have that same need in our neighborhood.*

That stirring is the beginning of the next Circle. The next Quest. The next transformation.

---

## Share Generously

Do not hoard your stories. They belong to the movement.

Share them in your Circle's journal. Share them with neighboring Circles. Offer them to the [[Commons]] so that Circles across the world can learn from your experience. Tell them at gatherings. Tell them to the people you are inviting.

Every story of transformation — honestly told, freely shared — becomes a seed that can take root anywhere the soil is ready.

The Great Game advances not only through action, but through the telling of what that action made real.

**Tell the story. [[Pass The Flame]].**
