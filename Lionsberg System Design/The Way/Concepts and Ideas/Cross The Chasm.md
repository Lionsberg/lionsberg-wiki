# Cross The Chasm
In the [[Lionsberg System]], "The Chasm" refers to the Gap between [[Where We Are]] and [[Where We Are Going]].  

It refers to the Gap between [[Who We Are]] and [[Who We Are Becoming]].  

It is a visual imagery that empowers us to grapple with the worst and most brutal facts / realities / elements of our current State... as well as the best and highest facts / realities / elements / possibilities of What Might Be / How High We Might Be Able To Go.
