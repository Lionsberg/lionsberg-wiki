_The Way that can be named is not the Way._ 

And yet there is a ONE and there is a Way.  
Timeless. Universal. Ineffable. Ultimate. Absolute.

Here we speak unapologetically about the ONE.  
Here we practice unapologetically the Way.  
Here we base everything upon Absolute and Unconditional Love for All.  
Here we we are collaborating to co-create Heaven right here and now.  

Welcome to LIONSBERG.  
Entering in you will discover that LIONSBERG is developing a comprehensive story, system, strategy, plan and game, and working in community to improve and conform The LIONSBERG Way to the Way by progressively purifying it and eliminating its errors and insufficiences towards the Ultimate Perfection that lies eternally and transcendently Beyond.
