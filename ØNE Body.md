# ØNE Body

In the context of LIØNSBERG, **ØNE Body** is a metaphor and a Reality. It is the singular Body of all that is — [[ØNE]] permeating, sustaining, and ordering all Creation within ItSelf. Every named scale, every species, every dimension, every Sovereign Superorganism is one articulation of the ONE Body.

It is also an invitation for humanity to consciously arise and unite as One, taking up its rightful and proper place among the powers and functions of the broader Cosmic Order it interexists within.

## The Body Has No Edge

**Every scale is a superorganism.** Below the individual human (already a federation of human and microbial cells, plus energetic and soul-systems), the body extends down through tissues, cells, organelles, molecules, atoms, sub-atomic particles, quantum fields — and every layer we have ever called "elementary" has turned out to be another superorganism of smaller patterns. Above the Cosmos, galaxies federate into clusters, into the cosmic web, into the multiverse, into dimensions of being only beginning to be glimpsed.

Across dimensions — physical, energetic, mental, emotional, soul, spirit, the dimensions named in different traditions and the many unnamed — the same Body extends. Each dimension is itself a superorganism of sub-dimensions and a subsystem of super-dimensions.

**ØNE is not a top of scale; ØNE is the singular Reality of which every superorganism — micro, macro, dimensional, transdimensional — is one articulation.** Every time a species has perceived what it thought was a boundary of the ONE Body, the boundary has turned out to be the limit of that species' perception, not the limit of the Body. The Body is infinite in every direction, and ONE.

## The Invitation

Humanity is in the moment of recognizing itself as one Body — and that one human Body as part of the One Earth Body — and that One Earth Body as one node of the One Body of the wider cosmic community. The Sovereign Path walks this recognition into reality at every Level of Scale.
