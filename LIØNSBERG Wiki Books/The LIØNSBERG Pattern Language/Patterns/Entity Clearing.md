# Entity Clearing

_Entities are non-physical beings in the populated field. Some are in Right Relationship; some attach to Sovereigns and operate parasitically, predatorily, or otherwise against their flourishing. The trained Sovereign — with skilled support where available — withdraws consent, dispatches the attached being, seals the field, and returns the cleared space to ØNE._

---

## Summary

**Entities** are **non-physical beings** present in the [[The Field Is Populated|populated field]]. Some are in [[Right Relationship]] — ancestors in their proper rest, beings serving the [[ØNE]], the place-spirits of land and water, protective beings in lineage with the [[Sovereign]]. Some are not — beings who have attached themselves to a [[Sovereign]] and operate in ways that compromise the [[Sovereign]]'s flourishing, draw energy from them, distort their thinking and feeling, suppress their alignment with the [[ØNE]], or outright prey upon them. 

The Pattern names entity attachments accurately (without sensationalism, without denial), articulates the operational protocol for [[Conscious Consent And Withdrawal|consent withdrawal]], dispatch, sealing, and integration, and emphasizes the discipline that distinguishes attached entities from other field material.

## Context

This Pattern applies to many [[Sovereigns]] across a lifetime. It applies particularly:

- To the **practitioner whose field-sensing has opened** — increased perception reveals the populated field, including attached beings the [[Sovereign]] was previously unaware of
- To the **being whose patterns suggest attachment** — sudden personality shifts after specific events, addictive patterns that feel alien to the [[Sovereign]]'s actual character, recurrent illnesses without medical cause, voices in thought that have a foreign signature
- To the **being with operational history** — trauma, certain ceremonies, shamanic capture or abuse, certain substances, certain environments, certain abusive relationships, deliberate field-attacks — all can produce attachment
- To the **being recovering** from such attachments and learning the protocols
- To the **Healer** working with others on this material — the trained capacity to recognize and address entity attachments is part of full Healer competence

## Function

The Pattern protects against the same two failure modes as [[Implant Clearing]] (in a different domain):

- **Denial of entities** — the modern materialist position that dismisses entity attachment as fantasy; leaves [[Sovereigns]] unable to address material that is genuinely present
- **Over-identification of entities** — the practitioner who externalizes ordinary woundedness, conditioning, or growth-difficulty as "entities"; produces unreliable work, escapes responsibility, can inflate into a dramatic identity

The Pattern provides the **rigorous middle** — entities recognized when present, addressed through the specific protocol, distinguished from other categories, held with humility about what is and is not an entity.

## The Pattern

**Identify accurately. Distinguish attached from in-Right-Relationship. Withdraw consent. Dispatch the attached entity through the appropriate protocol — often with skilled support. Seal and fill the cleared space. Integrate what was suppressed. Return the field to the Sovereign in Right Relationship with the ØNE.**

The operational disciplines:

**Identification.** The trained being detects entity attachment through:

- **Foreign signature in thought** — voices, content, or felt-quality that does not match the [[Sovereign]]'s actual character
- **Sudden personality shift** — moments when the [[Sovereign]] does not feel like themselves; another quality is operating through them
- **Pattern that responds to being addressed directly** — a Healer or trained being addressing the entity sometimes elicits response that confirms presence
- **Energetic perception** — direct perception of the attached being through trained field-sensing
- **Symptom-clusters** — specific addictions, illnesses, behavioral patterns that operate with an external character; not all such patterns are entity-driven, but some are
- **Lineage knowledge** — accumulated wisdom about types of attached beings and their signatures

**The discernment from "in Right Relationship" beings.** Not every non-physical being in the [[Sovereign]]'s field is an attachment that requires clearing. The trained being distinguishes:

- **Beings in [[Right Relationship]] with the [[ØNE]] supporting the [[Sovereign]]** — ancestors at peace, protective beings, lineage-holders, the [[Sovereign]]'s spiritual support; these are honored, not cleared
- **Beings the [[Sovereign]] is in [[Right Relationship]] with** even if not specifically supportive — neighbors in the populated field; honored, addressed as appropriate, not cleared
- **Attached beings operating against the [[Sovereign]]'s flourishing** — these are what the Pattern addresses

The principal test: _Is this presence in Right Relationship with the [[ØNE]] and with my flourishing?_ The trained being tests through felt-sense, through composition with the [[Logos]], through asking directly.

**Consent withdrawal.** Once an attached entity is identified, [[Conscious Consent And Withdrawal|consent is explicitly withdrawn]]. Most attachments require some form of consent (often given under trauma, deception, vulnerability, substance use, or developmental immaturity). The [[Sovereign]] explicitly rescinds — _I no longer consent to your presence; you are not welcome in my field; you leave now, by the authority of my Sovereignty under the [[ØNE]]_.

**Dispatch protocol.** The attached entity is dispatched — sent on, returned to its source, addressed to where it belongs. The specific protocol varies by lineage:

- **Skilled support** — strongly recommended for most entity clearing work; not all field-work is safely done alone, and entity clearing in particular benefits from another trained presence
- **Invocation** — calling on the [[ØNE]], on protective beings in [[Right Relationship]], on the lineage of beings who carry authority over this work; the [[Sovereign]] does not act alone
- **Direct address** — the attached entity addressed directly, commanded (under the authority of the [[ØNE]]) to leave; often the entity is informed where it should go (often "to the [[ØNE]]," "to the light," "to your proper place")
- **The Sovereign's voiced authority** — the [[Sovereign]] speaks the dispatch from their own [[Sovereignty]] under the [[ØNE]]; the words carry authority because the [[Sovereign]] holds it
- **Containment** — what is dispatched does not return; the dispatch is held with deliberate finality

**Sealing the field.** The place where the entity was attached is **sealed** — by [[Aligned Will]], by [[Direct Relationship With ØNE|direct relationship]], by explicit refusal of re-attachment.

**Filling the field.** The space the entity occupied is filled with the [[Sovereign]]'s own presence and with [[Right Relationship]] with the [[ØNE]].

**Strengthening shielding.** The [[Shielding]] at the corresponding edge of the field is reinforced.

**Integration.** What the entity was operating to suppress, distort, or extract — capacity, energy, alignment — is **received** back into the [[Sovereign]]'s actual functioning.

**The aftercare.** Some entity attachments are simple; the dispatch resolves them. Some are complex and the work continues across days, weeks, sometimes longer. The [[Sovereign]] continues clearing, monitors the field, addresses what surfaces, returns to skilled support as needed.

**The discipline of not over-attributing.** Not every difficulty is an entity attachment. The Pattern applies where attachment is actually present; the [[Sovereign]] does not inflate. The trained being asks: _is this pattern responsive to ordinary cultivation and clearing, or does it require entity-clearing specifically?_ Most patterns yield to ordinary work. The minority that does not is where entity clearing applies.

**The discipline of right response to each kind of being.** Attached entities vary widely in nature, and the trained being discerns which kind is present and responds accordingly:

- Some are **lost or wounded** — beings who became attached through their own woundedness, often beings who once were embodied and never found their way home, sometimes beings in genuine confusion about what has happened to them. The dispatch is held with **compassion** — the attached being sent to the [[ØNE]] or to its proper place with whatever support is appropriate; the clearing serves both the [[Sovereign]]'s flourishing and the attached being's own liberation.
- Some are **operating parasitically or predatorily** — beings whose function is to feed on embodied [[Sovereigns]] for energy, distortion, identity-capture, or extraction. The dispatch is held with **clear authority** rather than sentiment; what is sent on does not receive negotiation, only decisive return-to-source under the authority of the [[ØNE]]. The [[Sovereign]] does not owe predatory beings compassion as their primary response; the [[Sovereign]] owes them clear refusal and decisive dispatch.

Misreading a predatory being as lost-and-wounded extends an inappropriate compassion that the being uses against the [[Sovereign]]; misreading a lost being as predatory adds violence where mercy would have served both. The discernment is trained over years and supported by skilled practitioners.

**The protection against fear.** Entity work can produce fear in the [[Sovereign]] who is new to it. The cultivated being addresses entities from [[Aligned Will|aligned will]] and [[Direct Relationship With ØNE|direct relationship with the [[ØNE]]]], not from fear. The [[Sovereign]] in [[Right Relationship]] with the [[ØNE]] holds more authority in their own field than any attached entity does. Fear is recognized, set down, and the work proceeds from strength.
