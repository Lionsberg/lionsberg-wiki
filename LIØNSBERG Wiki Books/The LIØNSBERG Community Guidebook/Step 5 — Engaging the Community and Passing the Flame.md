# Step 5 — Engaging the Community and Passing the Flame

*A fire that does not spread, dies. A tree that bears no fruit has not yet fulfilled its nature.*

> **The work of this step has two movements.** First, broaden from the first 3% to the whole community. Second — the truest test of a living Company — help **birth the next one.** A body that cannot reproduce is not yet fully alive.

---

## Growth Is Not Replication

Hold this distinction, because nearly everything in this step turns on it:

- **Growth** adds members to your Community Company. It makes your Company *stronger.*
- **Replication** creates *new* Companies and Circles, each sovereign and complete, each carrying the full pattern. It makes the movement *wider.*

Both are necessary. But only multiplication reaches every neighborhood, every village, every town. A single Company that grows without ever replicating eventually strains, centralizes, and stalls. A Company that replicates seeds a forest. **Measure your success not by how large your Company has grown, but by how many federated Companies and Circles you have helped to bring alive.**

## Movement One — Broadening to the Whole Community

Your 300 were never the destination. They are the seed crystal. Once they are awake and working, the community follows — the committed few awaken the first few percent, and the first few percent awaken the rest. This broadening does not happen through programs, campaigns, or marketing. It happens through the **organic multiplication of living Circles**, each carrying the complete pattern, connected by trust.

Three things carry it:

- **Multiply your Circles.** As a Circle approaches ten or twelve members, it prepares to divide, and splits into two sovereign Circles before it exceeds sixteen. This is not loss; it is reproduction. One becomes two, two become five, until the whole community is laced with self-governing cells.
- **Become visible.** A Circle that stays hidden stays isolated. Share each Circle's name, its Quest, its general location, and a way to reach it in your community's shared directory — its [[Commons]]. Make it easy for the next person to find a fire already burning near them.
- **Connect and collaborate.** Circles find one another, coordinate Quests where one's strength meets another's need, share resources, and celebrate together. Collaboration is not merger — each Circle keeps its sovereignty, its own [[Field of Agreements]], its own inner life. Two whole cells working side by side. The structure that holds them together is the **Circle of Circles**: coordinators who serve and rotate, never rule, coordinating only what genuinely needs coordinating.

By the time the Old World's failures become undeniable, the New is already functional in your community — not as a plan, but as a living infrastructure of mutual aid, governance, and care that people can simply *step into.*

## Movement Two — Passing the Flame to the Next Community

This is not an optional final flourish. It is the culmination of the whole Path, and the proof that your Company has matured. A Community Company ready to pass the flame is one whose people have internalized the pattern, completed real work, and have relationships reaching beyond their own community.

The handoff has a clear shape:

1. **Identify your two or three — and act quickly.** Name the people, in a neighboring community, who are already awake and already reaching for this. Write the names down. Do not let the impulse cool; pass the seed forward within days, not someday.
2. **Share the invitation.** A single link to the Golden Seed — the core readings — and, more powerfully, *your own story.* Let the fire in your lived experience speak. You are not recruiting; you are introducing someone already-ready to a Way that fits what they already feel.
3. **Help them form their own sovereign body.** This is the crucial move: the new Company is **not a branch of yours, not a chapter, not a franchise.** It is a complete, self-governing expression of the same pattern — with its *own* name, its *own* Field of Agreements, its *own* Quests, its *own* people and place. Walk beside them through their founding (which is **[[Step 1 — Forming a Steering Committee|Step 1]]**, beginning again), and be present at their first gathering if they ask.
4. **Federate.** Your Company and the new one begin coordinating as peers — sharing learnings, pooling resources for larger Quests, establishing agreements at the next level up. This is the doorway to **[[Step 6 — Collective Governance of the Cosmic Superorganism]]**.
5. **Repeat.** Once the new Company finds its rhythm and completes its first work, *it* passes the flame again.

You are the link between the community that lit you and the community you will light. You carry the complete seed, adapted to a new voice, new people, a new place. This is the oldest pattern of life: a living thing reproducing itself.

## The Pattern in a Box

To make replication easy, the federation stewards an array of **shared, packaged solutions** in the Commons — tested ways of standing up a community enterprise, a food system, a governance structure, a healing practice — freely provided, ready to be localized. A new Company should never have to start from scratch; it draws down what the first communities already proved, adapts it to its own soil, and feeds its own learnings back. This is how the **100th community benefits from the wisdom of the first 99** — and the engine behind it is built in the next step.

## What Success Looks Like

You have completed Step 5 when you can point to:

- [ ] Circles **multiplying** across your community, not just your original Company growing.
- [ ] Circles **visible to one another** through a shared directory, and collaborating across their boundaries.
- [ ] A **Circle of Circles** coordinating the whole — serving, rotating, never ruling.
- [ ] At least one **new Community Company** you have helped birth elsewhere as a sovereign peer.
- [ ] That new Company **beginning to pass the flame itself.**

When the fire is leaping from community to community without your hand on every spark, you have done the deepest work this guidebook asks. What remains is to weave all these sovereign fires into one body.
