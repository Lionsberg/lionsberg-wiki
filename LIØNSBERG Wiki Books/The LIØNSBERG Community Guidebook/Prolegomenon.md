## What, Exactly, Is a Community Company?

A **Community Company** is the **self-governing body of a local community, united in service of The Goal.** It is defined by what it *is for* and *who it answers to*, not by any particular legal form.

Three things make a Community Company what it is:

1. **A People.** A bounded local community — as a working unit, often **roughly 10,000 people** (a town, a district, a cluster of villages, a neighborhood-of-neighborhoods). Small enough to know itself; large enough to meet most of its own needs. This is the basic self-governing cell of a new civilization. *(See "The Fractal" below for how cells federate outward and upward.)*

2. **A Purpose and Driver.** A single, sovereign purpose and need calling it into being: **throughput of The Goal** for that community — *the total integrated wellbeing, development, right relationship, and flourishing of all generations of life* in that place.

3. **Five Emergent Properties.** The moment its founding members voluntarily associate, five things come into being together — and *naming and tending them* is what turns a gathering into a Company:
   - a **[[Domains of Responsibility and Authority|Domain of Responsibility and Authority]]** — what this body is responsible for, and what it has authority over;
   - a **[[Selectively Permeable Membrane]]** — who is in, how members join and leave, what crosses the boundary and on what terms;
   - a **[[Field of Agreements]]** — the shared governance, freely consented to: purpose, principles, decisions, commitments;
   - a **[[Commons]]** — the shared resources the community pools and stewards together;
   - a **Federated Ecosystem** — the interexistence and right relationship of this Company with all others, so that no cell of the body stands alone.

**A Community Company is sovereign first, and structured second.** It does not need anyone's permission to exist. It begins the day its founders agree to voluntarily associate in service of **The Goal** — long before any paperwork. Legal personhood and form *emerges when the work requires it*. When it needs to hold money, sign contracts, limit liability, or interface with the existing legal world, a legal person is born and federated.

## A Continuously Improving Superorganism

To ensure that no one is involuntarily left behind, let us imagine the entire planet covered in such communities, each embarking on **a shared journey** towards flourishing and abundant life.

While there are indeed uniquenesses to each place, the vast majority of basic desires and needs are **common to all.**

_We all Value the same basic things._

Love. Belonging. Water. Food. Shelter. Clothing. Learning. Economy. Healing. Wellness. Spirituality. Regeneration. Justice. Meaning. Purpose. Governance.

Now imagine a world in which the very best **shared solutions** to these **universal desires and needs** were continuously improved and shared by each community.

In this world, the 100th community to form a **Community Company** would benefit from the wisdom, learning, and best practices of each of the first 99.

Each time a new solution was discovered, it would be "pushed" up to **The Commons**, so that it could be "pulled" down, localized, and adapted by any community that so desired.

The end result is **a shared planetary superorganism** whose purpose is to co-create Heaven On Earth.
