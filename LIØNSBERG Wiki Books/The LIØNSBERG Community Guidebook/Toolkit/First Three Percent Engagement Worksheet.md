# First Three Percent Engagement Worksheet

*Find the first to rise. Invite them. Track the rising.*

> Companion to **[[Step 3 — Engaging The First Three Percent]]**. The canon names the 3% beautifully but never tells you how to actually go find your 300. This worksheet does. Work it as a Steering Committee, and keep it living.

---

## The Target, Made Concrete

For a community of **10,000**, your first 3% is **~300 people.** But you do not invite 300 at once. You reach them in **waves of resonance**:

| Wave | Who | Rough size | How you reach them |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Seed** | The Steering Committee | 4–7 | Already here |
| **First Circle** | People each founder already trusts | ~30 | Personal invitation, one conversation each |
| **Circle of Circles** | The people *they* trust | ~100 | Each new person opens their own network |
| **The 3%** | The wider already-ready | ~300 | Gatherings, story, the flame spreading on its own |

You are not building an audience of 300. You are lighting **~30 fires that each light their own.**

## Part 3 — The Invitation Itself

A good invitation does three things: **names the urgency, offers the Way, and asks for a yes to one small next step** (come to a gathering — not "join forever").

> *"‹Name›, something is emerging that I think you would resonate with. A few of us are building a way for our community to actually govern and provide for itself. I thought of you immediately. Would you be willing to join one of our upcoming gatherings?"*

Adapt to your own voice and each person. Sincerity works a lot better than scripts.
