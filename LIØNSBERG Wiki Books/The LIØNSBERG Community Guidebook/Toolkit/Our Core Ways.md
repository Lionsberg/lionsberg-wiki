# Our Core Ways — The Handful of Things We Do the Same Way

*Everything is a process. Write down the few that matter, do them together, and improve them forever.*

> A community that keeps its know-how only in people's heads loses it the day someone leaves, and quietly does the same thing ten different ways in ten different corners. **Core Ways** are the short, written descriptions of the handful of processes that actually carry your Company — so the work is teachable, repeatable, improvable, and shareable. This is the LIØNSBERG conviction that *everything is a process,* made practical. Companion to **[[Step 4 — Learning to Play The New Game]]** and the canon's [[Everything Is A Process]].

---

## Find Your Vital Few

Do not document everything; you will drown, and no one will read it. Name the **five to ten processes that, if done well and the same way every time, hold your Company together.** Most communities' core ways are some of these:

- How we **welcome a new member** (see [[Member Onboarding Worksheet]]).
- How we **run a gathering** (see [[The Rhythm of Gatherings]]).
- How we **choose and close a Quest** (see [[From Driver to Quests]]).
- How we **steward money** through the [[Where Value Flows|value waterfall]].
- How we **make a decision** ([[Consent Governance One-Pager|by consent]]) and **resolve a conflict**.
- How we **welcome, seat, and thank** those who hold the Seats for the living world and the unborn.

## Write Each One Simply

For each Core Way, capture only **the vital few steps** — the handful that actually matter, not every keystroke. If it runs longer than a single page, you are over-documenting; cut back to the bones a newcomer would actually need.

> **‹Name of the Way›**
> *Why it matters:* ‹one line ›
> *The steps:* 1. ‹ › 2. ‹ › 3. ‹ ›
> *Who holds it:* ‹ ›

## Then: Followed by All

Once the body has agreed a Core Way, **everyone does it that way.** This is not to crush creativity — it is so the Company is coherent, so a newcomer can learn fast, and so that improvements *compound* instead of scattering. If someone believes there is a better way, they do not quietly fork it in their own corner — they raise it as an [[Issues & Tensions|issue]], the body discerns, and the *written* Way is updated for all. Coherence by consent, not by command.

## And: Continuously Improve

Every Core Way is provisional — *good enough for now,* never sacred. Revisit them as you grow. When you discover a better way, change the writing, and **share it up to the [[Step 6 — Collective Governance of the Cosmic Superorganism|Commons]]** so that other Companies inherit your hard-won wisdom and never have to learn it the painful way. A solution found once, anywhere, should bless everyone.

---

To tend your Core Ways is to live the whole System: that everything is a process, that the body holds its wisdom in common, that we improve forever rather than arrive, and that what one community learns, all may receive.
