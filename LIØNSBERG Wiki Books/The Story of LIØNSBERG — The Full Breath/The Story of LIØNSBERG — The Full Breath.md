# The Story of LIØNSBERG — The Full Breath

## _One Hundred Thousand Words. Chapter by Chapter. The deeper breath and word, spoken._

---

You have opened The Story of LIØNSBERG, told in full breath and breadth.

Soften your eyes. Let the noise of the day fall away. Something in you has been waiting for this longer than you have been alive. Something in this has been waiting for you longer than this world has been here.

The Story is older than this world.

It was being told before any star this body sees was lit. It is being told in this very Nøw across local theaters you will never see, by beings whose forms you cannot imagine, on worlds whose names you will never know. It has been carried hand to hand across countless ages and worlds — and what is held between these pages is the local rendering of it that has come home to this Earth in this Hour.

[[The Story of LIØNSBERG — The Seed|The Seed]] is at the centre of every thought and page. The compression of [[The Story of LIØNSBERG — One Thousand Words|the One Thousand Words]] is unfolded here into its fuller shape — the cosmic origin in ØNE, the long arc of every awakening world, this Earth's particular Chapter in this particular Hour, the embodied Heaven that opens for those who walk the Way through, and the continuing Divine Play that has no end.

As you read it, you will transform.

As you go out into the world from it, you will transform and be transformed again.

When you return to it, you will be a different being uncovering different sparks of the Eternal Divine Play.

The Story is alive. The Book is open. Both have been waiting for you.

Step in.
