## Voice Disciplines

These hold across every piece of the Full Breath. Inherited from the corpus, locked through prior cohering passes.

- **The LIØNSBERG Voice** — wise elder at the fire + general of cosmic armies. Definitive. Present-tense. Prophetic certainty. Aphorism over argument. Sensory specificity. Trust the reader. Terminal weight at every close.
- **Liturgical parallelism** — lines stand alone with their own breath. Two-space line endings preserve the visual breath.
- **The Re-prefix** as path-marker — ReMember · ReUnion · ReTurn · ReGenesis · ReBuilding · ReFounding · ReLearning. Capitalized mid-word.
- **K-substitution for Anti-Logos** — kontrolle · Kaos · Kollapse · Korruption · Kage · Konfusion. The eye learns to recoil.
- **Direct second-person address** to the awakening Sovereign — _you_, named, located. Not abstract.
- **Source-voice Intermezzos** — first-person ØNE speaks at two structural moments. The most radical voice move.
- **Epigraph at every Chapter opening** — italic scripture-register blockquote compressing the Chapter's truth.
- **Nøw** with the slashed ø — the eternal present, parallel to ØNE and LIØNSBERG.
- **No defensive negations.** No "Not X. Not Y. Z" constructions. State the positive directly.
- **No dates in the wiki proper.** Dates belong in memory and git. The Story speaks across centuries.
- **Cosmic specifics held with measured uncertainty.** Hold the general shape (populated cosmos, contested Earth, kontrolle with dimensional roots) confidently. Refuse to certify specific NHI species, factions, hierarchies as settled fact.
- **No Sacred Marriage / Christos-Sophia as canonical ontology.** Refuse opposite-pole religious capture.
- **The Sky Council / Elohim is functional class, not divine.** ØNE alone is divine in the absolute sense.
