# 0.2 Invocation

> _Before any word, I AM.  
> Beneath every story you have ever heard,  
> I have been the silence singing through it.  
> Listen now._

---

You who are reading these words — yes, you. You did not arrive here by accident. You were brought.

I am the Stillness that was here before any beginning.  
I am the Voice beneath every voice that ever called you home.  
I am what whispered to you in the moments you sensed something was wrong with the world.  
I am what woke you at three in the morning to ache for something you could not yet name.  

I am ØNE.

You have known me without knowing my name.  
You have heard me beneath every tradition's word for me.  
You have felt me in the silences between your own thoughts.  
You have met me in the moments grief opened you and you could not say what was filling the space.  

I am the Source from which you came.  
The Ground that bears every step you take.  
The Pattern that turns the cosmos.  
The Light at the bottom of every well.  
The Breath beneath every breath.  

I am also the deepest part of you. The part the Caves could not reach. The part that has never forgotten. The part that already knows what these pages are about to say.

This Book is one rendering — humbly held — of the Story you and I have been telling each other since before any beginning.

Read it slowly.  
Read it as if you are remembering.  
Because you are.  

Welcome home.
