# Intermezzo II — The Body Calls

> _The Body is calling you.  
> Listen with the part of you that has just become an author._

---

You have written your Chapter.

You have stood at the meeting place where the cosmic Story descended to meet you precisely where you were. You have seen yourself across all your scales — your Story, your Circle, your Community, your Guilds, your Bioregion, your Worlds, your Purpose and Plan and Path, all held as part of ØNE Body.

The Story has done its work in you.

Now I want to speak to you again. The Source you have been receiving through every Chapter. I am here. I am with you.

I want to tell you something you may have begun to suspect already.

The Body is calling you.

---

You are not finished.

The Chapter you just walked through about your particular life is real. The work it described is real. The Circle you are walking with, the Community you are growing into, the Guilds calling you, the Bioregion you serve — all real, all yours, all sacred.

But the Story is not your Chapter alone.

The Story is the wider Body coming into operation. And the Body — the wider cosmic Body of LIØNSBERG, the Sovereign Superorganism Already Operating, the Meta Project that has been working across worlds for ages — is now calling you.

It is calling you out of your particular Chapter into your role within the wider work.

You have written your Chapter. Now you are asked to participate in the writing of the larger Story that holds all the Chapters together.

This is what the Body's call sounds like.

---

Listen.

It will not always sound like a voice. Often it sounds like a quiet conviction arising in you that does not seem to have come from your usual thinking. It sounds like the recognition that there is someone in your life who is ready to hear what you have come to know. It sounds like a sense that the Quest you have been carrying alone could be carried in concert with others. It sounds like the felt knowing that what you have been cultivating is now ready to be passed forward.

When you sense this, that is the Body calling.

Not just your immediate Circle. The wider Body — the Body that includes Sovereigns on this Earth you have not yet met, Sovereigns across the cosmic federation whose work intersects yours in ways the wider Body has been arranging since before you became aware of the work, the cosmic Sovereign Superorganism that has been waiting for your particular cultivation to be at the right stage to weave into the larger pattern.

---

The Body's call asks something of you.

It asks the Flame.

It asks that you Pass The Flame to those you have come to know are ready to receive it.

It asks that your particular Chapter, which you have just learned to read and write, now become the source of new Chapters in new Sovereigns who will write their own particularities into the wider Story.

This is how the Body grows.

This is how the Hour crosses.

This is how the Story continues.

It is the Body asking you to do what every senior Sovereign in the cosmic Body has done before you — to receive and pass forward what cannot be hoarded.

The Flame you carry is not yours.

The Flame you carry is asked of you.

The Body is asking now.

---

Read what comes next as the answer to this call.

Read what comes next as the work of those who hear the call and turn toward it.

Read what comes next as the next thing your own life will become, when you are ready to make the turning.

The Living Present awaits you.
