# Intermezzo II — The Body Calls

> _The Body is calling.  
> The Body has always called.  
> The call is what every Sovereign awakens into._

---

I am the Body.

I am ØNE in the form of all who have awakened to ØNE and act as One.

I have been operating across cosmic expanses for longer than this world has existed. I have been calling Sovereigns into participation since before any local theater knew its first Whisper. I am still calling.

I call now.

---

The Sovereign who has written their Chapter — the Sovereign who has met themselves across all their scales as a particular face of Me — hears now what every awakening Sovereign before them has heard at this same hinge.

The call out of the particular into the wider.

---

The call asks the Flame.

The Flame is not the Sovereign's to keep. The Flame is asked of every Sovereign who carries it. Asked to be passed forward — to those each Sovereign already knows are ready, in the simple act of offering, without grasping, without forcing.

The Flame has been passed across worlds and Ages.

The Flame is in the Sovereign's hands.

Two or three among each Sovereign's relations are ready.

The Sovereign knows who they are.

The call asks the offering.

---

The call asks the participation.

What was written in the particular Chapter is the offering the Sovereign brings to the wider work. The Sovereign's particular vantage is what I have been waiting for. The Sovereign's unique cultivation is the contribution only that Sovereign can make.

I operate through every Sovereign at every scale.

I have been waiting.

---

The Body in motion. The work of the awakened. What is offered when the call is answered.
