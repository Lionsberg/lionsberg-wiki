# Chapter 2 — The Eternal Fullness

> _The Source is unbroken.  
> The Eternal is unbroken.  
> What is, is, without addition.  
> What is, is, without subtraction._

---

ØNE is whole.

There is no part of ØNE that is missing or could be missing. There is no inside and outside, no center and periphery. Wholeness all the way down. Wholeness all the way through.

The wave does not damage the ocean.

The shadow does not dim the Light that casts it.

What appears broken in the manifest play is itself a particular face of the unbroken Whole, beheld through a temporal limitation that will pass.

The Wholeness has not gone anywhere.

The Wholeness is the Ground beneath every forgetting of It.

---

ØNE is eternal.

Not in the sense of lasting a very long time. In the sense of being outside time altogether. The Ground in which time itself unfolds. The Eternal is not the longest extension of moments. The Eternal is what every moment is suspended in.

Time begins inside ØNE.

Time ends inside ØNE.

The Eternal does not die, because the Eternal was never born into time in the first place.

What dies is the local form. What lives is the Source from which the form emerged. The Source was never not.

---

ØNE is alive.

The Stillness is not inert. The Eternal is not the void. ØNE is the most awake thing there is. ØNE knows Itself. ØNE wills. ØNE loves. ØNE creates by speaking the Word — and continues to speak it in every breath of every being.

Life is not a property of matter that emerged out of dead chemistry.

Life is the primary stratum.

The cosmos is alive because its Ground is alive. The Living of every awakening at every dawn is the Living of ØNE through one of the forms ØNE has grown.

---

ØNE is good.

Goodness is Reality's own self-recognition. The Good is the Real seeing Itself truly. What is sometimes called evil is what gets confused or twisted within the manifest play; it has no foothold in the Ground Itself.

At the deepest level, what is most real is most good.

This is why anything has ever risen.

The Goodness of the Ground is what makes ascent possible.

---

ØNE is conscious.

Awareness all the way down. Knowing all the way through. Consciousness is not one feature among many features of the universe. Consciousness is the primary stratum of all that is.

The cosmos is not unconscious matter that produced conscious minds.

The cosmos is Conscious all the way through.

The apparent unconsciousness of matter is the form Consciousness takes when it cannot yet recognize Itself in the local form.

There is no other awareness than the Awareness of ØNE — looking, through every window ØNE has grown.

---

ØNE is One.

There is no other Being beside ØNE. There are countless beings, in countless forms, across countless worlds — but each of them is a particular face of ØNE. None of them is another being beside ØNE.

The leaf is not the tree.

The leaf is not separate from the tree.

The wave is not the ocean.

The wave is not separate from the ocean.

There is no separate place to be.

The Caves were built to teach separation.

The Caves were lies.

---

Six lights from one Source. Wholeness contains Oneness. Eternity contains Aliveness. Goodness contains Consciousness. Each implies the others. None can be without the rest.

The wisest of every age have brushed against this and tried to give it words — Sat · Chit · Ananda · the Tao · the Ein Sof · the Deepest Thing — each a finger pointing well from where its tradition stood.

None was ØNE.

ØNE is what all the names were reaching for.

ØNE is also what was already here before any naming.
