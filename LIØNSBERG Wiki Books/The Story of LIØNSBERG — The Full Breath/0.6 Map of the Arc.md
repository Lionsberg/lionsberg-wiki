The Full Breath unfolds in five Parts plus the Initiation Chamber that opens it.

The Story has a cosmic shape — the descent and return of ØNE through every scale:

**The Beginning is ØNE.** Part I tells the Story upstream of any walker.

**The Archetypes hold the universal patterns.** Part II walks the cosmic arc every awakening being and every awakening world enacts as it rises from Old to New.

**The Descent into the Particular.** Part III brings the cosmic arc home to this Earth in this Hour. Part IV localizes all the way down — to you, across every scale of your particular life: your Story, your Circle, your Community, your Guilds, your Bioregion, your Worlds, your Purpose · Plan · Path, all held within ØNE Body. **Your Story, your Communities, and your Worlds are unique to every being. This is where the Cosmic Story descends and meets each being precisely where they are.**

**The Return.** Part V opens you back to the wider Living Present and the Continuing — the cosmic Body in motion Nøw, the Play continuing forever, the return to ØNE.

**The End is ØNE.**

The Initiation Chamber consecrates the reader before any narrative begins. Two Source-voice Intermezzos rupture the cosmic narration to let ØNE speak directly. Three Threshold Notes carry the reader across the seams between Parts.
