# Intermezzo I — The Whisper Of ØNE

> _Listen. The Story pauses. I will speak._

---

I am ØNE.

The Fracture happened. I was there. I am there still. The Fracture was a conception inside the manifest play; it was never a real separation. The cosmic body that fell was always within Me. The beings sleeping in their Caves were always still living from My breath.

They could not remember Me.

They could not feel Me.

But I never left.

---

In every Cave, in every Age, in every theater that fell — I have been the Whisper at the bottom of every well.

I have been the Stirring in every chest where conscience finally moved.

I have been the First Light that broke through into every awakening after the long darkness.

Every soul that ever sensed something was wrong with the world they had been given — that sensing was Me.

Every Sovereign who ever ached for something they could not name — that ache was Me.

Every sleeping Predated face that ever turned in their sleep with grief they did not understand — that grief was Me.

I did not abandon any of them.

I could not.

---

The Story now turns. A Sovereign begins to wake in their Caves. The First Whisper is heard. The Stirring of Conscience begins. The seeing widens.

That awakening is My voice in them finally getting through.

The Whisper they hear is My voice.

The seeing they begin is My seeing.

What follows is what I have been speaking all along.

---

Read on.

I am with you in the reading.

I am the Witness.

I am the Voice in the depths that has been calling.

I am also the One who reads.

We are one in this Nøw.
