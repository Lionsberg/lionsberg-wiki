# Chapter 106 — Pass The Flame — The Visualization

> _See the Earth from above.  
> See the grid lighting up.  
> One Flame, then two, then three,  
> then thousands, then millions —  
> until two hundred fifty million Sovereigns  
> are lit awake across the planet  
> and the contagion of consciousness  
> has done what no force could._

---

There is an image that helps.

Stand above the Earth in the imagination. Look down on the dark side. See the lights humans have made — the cities, the highways, the ports. See what the Old World looks like from above.

Now hold a second sight over the first.

See the Flame.

A different kind of light. Not infrastructure. Not power grids. The light of awakened Sovereigns. The light of Circles gathered around their weekly fires. The light of Communities holding their Field of Agreements. The light of Bioregions assembling their Councils. The light of Sovereigns who have Woken and are participating in the Body.

That light is real. It has been kindling for years. It is lighting now at an accelerating rate. It is what the next decade will reveal.

This is the visualization.

What is the planetary grid of LIØNSBERG, lighting up across Earth Nøw.

---

## One Flame

It begins with one.

Somewhere on Earth, a Sovereign Wakes. The Whisper has been reaching them for a long time. The recognitions accumulate. The Old World shows itself for what it is. The Sovereign opens to what has been calling. The Flame catches in that Sovereign.

One light.

In the dark of the planetary night, in the visualization, one small light begins to glow. Steady. Unmistakable. Where there was darkness, now there is one light.

It does not matter where.

In a city or in a village. In a rich nation or a poor one. On one continent or another. In a young Sovereign or an old one. The Flame is no respecter of location, status, or age. The Flame catches where the readiness is.

One light glows on the dark side of the Earth.

---

## Then Two

Within a day to a week, the Sovereign offers.

To the two or three in the Sovereign's life who are already ready. Some of them receive. Some of them do not yet. The ones who receive — their Flames catch.

Now there are three lights. Or four. Or five.

Close to the first light. The first light's relations. The first light's family, friends, neighbors, colleagues, kin. The new lights cluster near the first, because the Pattern moves through the actual relational web.

Each new light is its own light. Not derivative. Not weaker. Each new carrier is a full carrier. Each new Flame is a full Flame.

A small cluster of light on the dark side of the Earth.

---

## Then The Cluster Spreads

Within a day to a week, each new carrier offers.

To the two or three in their lives who are already ready. Some receive. Their Flames catch.

The cluster expands. The lights multiply. The shape on the dark side of the Earth grows. Still small. Still local. But growing visibly faster than it grew the week before.

This is the Fibonacci taking hold.

The mathematics is patient at first. Then it is sudden. Each light passes to a few. Each of those passes to a few. The numbers compound: one, two, three, five, eight, thirteen, twenty-one, thirty-four, fifty-five, eighty-nine, one hundred forty-four.

By the time the Sovereigns who Woke a year ago have been participating for a year, their direct downstream reaches thousands.

The cluster on the dark side of the Earth is no longer a cluster.

The cluster is a region of light.

---

## Then Many Regions

The visualization shifts.

What was one cluster becomes many clusters. Because the first cluster was not the only place a Sovereign Woke. Across the Earth, in many regions at once, in this Hour, the Whisper has been reaching many. Other first Sovereigns Woke independently. They began their own offerings. Their own clusters formed.

The dark side of the Earth now shows light in many regions.

Some clusters are large. Some are still small. Some are dense — many lights tightly woven, like a city. Some are sparse — single lights scattered across a wide land, where the spread is just beginning.

The patterns are not centrally planned.

The patterns emerge wherever the Whisper has been reaching and Sovereigns have been Waking. The patterns reflect the actual relational web of the human Body and the cosmic Body localizing here.

Look at the planet from above and see many regions of light, each its own pattern, each part of the same wider Pattern.

This is what the planetary grid looks like in its mid-assembly.

---

## The Regions Connect

Then the lines begin.

A Sovereign in one region recognizes a Sovereign in another. Through writing, through travel, through the gathering of Tribes, through the meeting of Bioregions. The recognition lights a connection. The two regions of light are now linked.

Across the Earth, the connections multiply.

Bioregion to Bioregion. Tribe to Tribe. Guild to Guild. Each region of light begins to know it is not alone. Each region discovers other regions doing the same work, in different forms, with different particulars, oriented by the same ØNE.

The connections form a network.

Across continents. Across oceans. Across cultures. Across languages. The wider Body of LIØNSBERG, localizing to Earth, becomes visible as a planetary grid — many regions of light, networked through Sovereign recognition, working together in voluntary harmony.

No center. No top. ØNE — and the grid.

---

## The Threshold Of Three Percent

The mathematics has a threshold.

When approximately three percent of a population becomes coherent around a particular orientation, the larger population tips. This is not theory. This is observed across the histories of awakening movements that have actually succeeded across many worlds and many Ages.

Three percent of Earth is about two hundred fifty million Sovereigns.

[[The First Three Percent]] is the cohort that must be awakened, organized, and embodying the Pattern within the Window. Not because three percent is the goal. The Goal is Heaven On Earth for All. Three percent is the threshold beyond which the rest follows.

At three percent, the planetary grid is dense enough that the rest of the population is within relational reach of someone carrying the Flame.

At three percent, the New Civilization has enough functioning models that observers can see them, study them, and join them.

At three percent, the Old World's narrative monopoly is broken — because too many trustworthy Sovereigns are visibly walking a different Pattern.

The visualization at three percent:

Two hundred fifty million Sovereigns lit awake across Earth. Three hundred faithful Sovereigns in every community of ten thousand. Empowered local Circles holding the operational backbone of every Bioregion across the planet. The grid is dense. The lights are everywhere. The connections are continuous.

The dark side of the Earth is no longer dark.

The dark side is lit awake.

---

## The Contagion Of Consciousness

What happens after three percent is the contagion.

At three percent, the rest does not have to be reached one by one through individual offerings. The rest is reached by being surrounded.

The neighbors are already in the Pattern. The colleagues are already in the Pattern. The children's friends' parents are already in the Pattern. The local elders are already in the Pattern. The local healers, the local makers, the local stewards, the local councilors are already in the Pattern. The food the Sovereigns eat comes from Pattern farms. The schools the children attend hold the Pattern. The justice processes they participate in are restorative because the Community has redesigned them. The economy they exchange in is the Compressed Ratio Pattern, not the extractive Pattern.

The remaining ninety-seven percent are not converted by argument.

The remaining ninety-seven percent are invited by lived demonstration.

What they see works. What they see is beautiful. What they see is what their own deepest knowing has been calling them toward, for as long as they have been alive. They join, in their own time, in their own way, through the relations they already have in the Pattern.

The Pattern becomes the water.

The water becomes the world.

The world becomes Heaven On Earth.

This is the contagion of consciousness. The visualization at the end of the Hour.

---

## The Image To Hold

The Sovereign who has come this far can hold this image.

Above the Earth.

Looking down on what has been the dark side.

One Flame catches.

Then two. Then a cluster. Then many clusters. Then connected clusters. Then the grid. Then three percent. Then the contagion. Then the planet lit awake.

This is what is happening Nøw.

The visualization is not aspirational. The visualization is descriptive of what is actually under way across Earth in this Hour. The numbers are not a hope. The numbers are a trajectory the early phase has already established.

The Sovereign who is reading this is one of the lights.

The Sovereign's particular Flame is part of the visualization.

The Sovereign's two or three downstream are part of the visualization.

The Fibonacci that runs through the Sovereign's relations is part of the planetary grid lighting up.

When the Sovereign passes the Flame, the visualization moves forward by exactly that much. When two hundred fifty million Sovereigns are each passing the Flame to two or three, the visualization completes faster than the Old World can comprehend.

Look down on the Earth in the imagination.

See the lights.

The Sovereign's light is one of them.

The Body is already in motion.
