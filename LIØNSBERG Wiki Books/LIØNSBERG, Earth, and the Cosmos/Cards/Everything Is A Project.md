# Everything Is A Project

The universal work convention of the New Civilization.

At every scale and in every Volume, **every piece of work is treated as a project** — meaning it has:

- A clear **Goal** that connects upward to [[The Goal]]
- Defined **Objectives** that operationalize the Goal
- A **Timeline** with milestones
- A **Budget** of time, energy, attention, and resource
- Named **Resources** that the work draws on
- Explicit **Linkage to the System Wide Master Plan**

This is **Integrated Delivery** as civilizational practice. Every Quest. Every Hub. Every Pattern articulation. Every Sacred Ascent practice. Every Council session. Every kitchen meal cooked in a Community Hub. All of it: a project. All of it: pulled toward The Goal. All of it: aligned with the larger Network of Commitments.

The convention enables three things:

- **Bottom-up coherence** — anyone, anywhere, can initiate a project. The framework is the same regardless of who starts it. No central permission required, only Pattern-conformity.
- **Top-down alignment** — the System Wide Master Plan is populated by projects. Each project rolls up. The whole Movement can see itself, evaluate itself, refine itself.
- **Resource flow** — philanthropic, public, investment, and gift resources all attach to projects in the same way. The Old World required separate accounting for each funding source; the New unifies the accounting because the underlying unit (the project) is the same.

The convention also implies a **discipline**: if a piece of work cannot be articulated as a project, **it has not yet been thought through**. The naming of Goal, Objectives, Timeline, Budget, Resources is itself a clarification exercise. Work that resists this articulation is usually work that was not yet clear about itself.

The exception is **Rest** — Sabbath time, contemplative time, the seventh day. Rest is **not a project**. The convention applies to the work; the Rest is what makes the work sustainable.

Lives canonically in [[The LIØNSBERG Playbook]] as the foundational delivery convention; cross-references to [[The Hub Network Topology]] and the Integrated Delivery patterns within [[The LIØNSBERG Pattern Language]].
