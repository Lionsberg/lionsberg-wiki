- **What Patterns To Reach For** — the Pattern Cards most useful here ([[Integrated Delivery]], [[Networks of Commitments]], [[Quest]], [[Design To The Goal]], the Twelve Pillars Patterns)
- **What Other Sovereigns Have Found Hard** — premature scaling; building before remembering; abandoning practical work for vision
- **When You Are Ready For The Next Step** — how Movement 8 closes (the New visible in the Sovereign's life, ready to participate in the species-scale ReFounding)
- **If You Are Stuck** — what to do if the walking slows
