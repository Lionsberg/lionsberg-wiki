# The Time Horizons

_When the walk unfolds — the natural calendar from first minute through first year and beyond._

_One of the four frames of [[The LIØNSBERG Experience|The Experience]]._

---

The [[The 12 Stages of the Sovereign's Path|12 Stages]] tell the Sovereign _what_ unfolds. The Time Horizons tell them _when_.

These horizons are permissive, not prescriptive. Some Sovereigns walk in days what others walk in seasons. The Seed takes root in its own time. What follows describes the natural shape of the walk for most who carry it through. See [[The Solo Sovereign's Grace]] for the Sovereign who walks slowly.

---

## First Minute (0–60 seconds)

**Recognition.** The Sovereign arrives at the [[README|Golden Seed]] via a trusted person's link. They read the opening invocation. The Seed either resonates or it does not. There is no other test.

What can unfold inwardly: tingling, recognition, a sense of _"I already knew this."_ The body knowing before the mind explains.

---

## First Five Minutes

**Decision.** The Sovereign scans the README — the three things (Story · Pattern · Game), the stakes (the Moment of Millennia), the Sacred Invitation to [[The First Three Percent]].

This is the choice point. Cross the threshold, or close the tab. Both are sovereign. The Seed continues to travel either way.

---

## First Half Hour

**Orientation.** The Sovereign clicks `[[Welcome to LIØNSBERG]]`. They read the deeper invitation — The Ground ([[ØNE]]), The Pattern (the 12 Irreducible Elements), The Game (Circles, Quests, Rhythm), The Ascent.

By the end of this horizon, the Sovereign sees what this is. The shape is clear. The fit is felt.

---

## First Hour

**Practical Decision.** The Sovereign reads "Gather Your Circle" inside the Welcome. They begin identifying 2-3 trusted others mentally — the ones who are already awakening, already seeking, already ready.

The list begins to form. The Sovereign is now oriented and motivated. Whether they act immediately or sit with it longer is their own discernment.

---

## First Day (1–24 hours)

**Seed-Planting.** The Sovereign sits with what was received. They may return to favorite passages, read [[The Story of LIØNSBERG|The Story]] in full, or pause in stillness.

When ready — sometimes within the first day, sometimes after several days of integration — they reach out to those 2-3 trusted others. The message is simple:

> _"I just received something powerful. Would you read it with me this week?"_

A single link. No explanation required. Either it resonates with them or it does not. They will know within their own first minute. See [[The 1-Day to 1-Week Threshold]] for the resistance often encountered at this gate.

This is also the day **[[The Daily Way]]** can begin — even before the Circle is gathered. The morning stillness, the evening reflection, the simple rhythms.

---

## First Week

**Gather and Begin.** Those who resonated respond. The Circle of 3 to 12 sovereign souls is convened. They meet for 90 minutes — in person or by video — using [[The LIØNSBERG Quick Start Guide]] as the template.

Within this 90 minutes they consecrate the Circle, read aloud together, sense-make, choose the first seasonal Quest, set the weekly rhythm, and each member names the 2-3 they will invite next.

The Flame passes outward. The Circle is alive. The Sovereign's weight shifts from "alone" to "we."

For the Sovereign still reading alone — the Seed has not stopped working. See [[The Solo Sovereign's Grace]].

---

## First Month

**Establish Rhythm.** Four weekly Circle meetings. Working on the first seasonal Quest. **[[The Daily Way]]** practiced consistently — the morning stillness now habitual, the evening reflection a known ground.

Pattern Encounters arise when real questions surface — governance of the Circle, conflicts that need resolution, decisions about scope. The Sovereign and the Circle reach into [[The LIØNSBERG Pattern Language|the Pattern Language]] for the specific Patterns that answer specific questions.

Discipline is forming. The Way moves from intellect into body.

---

## First Season (~90 days, one solstice-or-equinox to the next)

**Bear Fruit.** The first Quest is completed — or honestly retrospected — before the next solstice or equinox. The Circle celebrates, reflects, and chooses the next Quest. See [[The LIØNSBERG Calendar]] for the seasonal turning points.

By this point, 1-2 [[Pass The Flame|Pass-the-Flame]] cycles are already underway. Some of the original Circle's members have gathered their own Circles. The Fibonacci begins.

The Game is no longer theory. Heaven has begun to manifest at small scale — measurably, locally, in the lives the Sovereigns actually live.

---

## First Year

**Stable Practice.** Four seasonal Quests complete. The Circle is robust. Sub-Circles have spawned through Pass-the-Flame. A Community of Circles is beginning to form — Circles that know each other, learn from each other, support each other.

[[The Sacred Ascent]] has opened for those called to deeper practice. Pattern fluency is established. The Sovereign is no longer learning the Way — they are teaching it, simply by living it.

Identity has shifted. The Sovereign _is_ now a Player.

---

## Beyond

**Convergence to Eternal Play.** The arc continues — Communities federate into Bioregions, Bioregions form the [[Planetary Grid]], [[The First Three Percent]] reach critical mass, [[Covenant and Jubilee — December 2033]] approaches and is consciously enacted, and the Game opens into the **Eternal Divine Play** across worlds, species, and ages.

The Sovereign's first year is the seed of an eternal walk.
