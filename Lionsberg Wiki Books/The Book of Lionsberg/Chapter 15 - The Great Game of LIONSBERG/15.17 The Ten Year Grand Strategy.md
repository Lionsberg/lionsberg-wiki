The ten year horizon unfolds in three phases:

**Phase One: The Seven Year Window of Crisis and Opportunity (2024-2030)**

This is the critical window. During these years, the Old World accelerates its collapse while the New Civilization is being grown from the ground up. Every person is invited — not by a stranger, but by someone they know and trust. Every community sees its three hundred emerge. Every bioregion begins to federate.

By the end of Phase One, the planetary grid is substantially in place. The New Civilization is functional — not on paper, but in practice. Communities are feeding themselves. Governance is self-organized. The living economy is circulating. The culture of the Game is undeniable.

**Phase Two: The Three Year Lag (2030-2033)**

Transformation is not instant. Each person who enters the Game needs approximately three years to move from awakening through healing to full empowerment. The final cohorts who join in 2030 complete their formation by 2033. During this phase, the New Civilization deepens, matures, and stabilizes.

**Phase Three: The New Era (2033 and Beyond)**

The New Civilization is no longer emerging — it is the default. The Old structures that remain are either transformed from within or allowed to fall away naturally. The Game continues — because the Game never ends. But it is no longer a race against collapse. It is the eternal practice of co-creating Heaven, cycle by cycle, season by season, generation by generation.

The cooperative movement: twenty-eight weavers in Rochdale, 1844. Three million cooperatives and one billion members today. Seven principles on one page.

The mathematics of organic growth are not fantasy. They are the most proven pattern in the history of human civilization. The only variable is whether enough of us will begin.
