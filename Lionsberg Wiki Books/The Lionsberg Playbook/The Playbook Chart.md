# The Playbook Chart

_The continuously-improving map of which Patterns compose which Plays. The shared canonical source for both [[The LIØNSBERG Playbook|Vol IV — The Playbook]] and [[The LIØNSBERG Pattern Language|Vol V Part — The Pattern Language]]. As Plays and Patterns mature, this Chart matures with them._

---

## What This Chart Is

The Playbook is a field music-book. The Plays are riffs. The Patterns are notes. **This Chart shows which notes are in which riff.**

Read top-to-bottom for **Plays and what they invoke**.  
Read across the **Patterns Index** below for **which Plays each Pattern serves** (reverse index).

The Chart is the **shared navigation tool**: when we author a new Pattern Card, we know which Plays it must serve. When we compose a new Play, we know which Pattern Cards it draws on. **No duplication. Maximum reuse. Living refinement.**

## How To Use This Chart

**Authoring a new Pattern Card.** Find which Plays invoke this Pattern in the Reverse Index above. Author the Pattern to serve all those invocation-contexts well. Each Pattern Card opens to many Plays; the Pattern must hold its wisdom in a way that each Play can compose from it.

**Composing a new Play.** Find the Patterns the Play needs in the Plays section above (or add a new row). The Play references each Pattern; the Pattern Cards deliver the wisdom; the Play orchestrates.

**Refining a Pattern.** When a Pattern's articulation deepens, all Plays that invoke it benefit automatically — because each Play references the canonical Pattern, not a duplicate.

**Refining a Play.** When the operational riff of a Play sharpens, the Play's composition (the Patterns it invokes) may change. The Chart updates.

**Discovering a missing Pattern.** If a Play needs a Pattern that doesn't yet exist, the Chart surfaces the gap. The Pattern becomes the next authoring target.

**Discovering a missing Play.** If a common operational need has no Play, the Chart surfaces the gap. The Play becomes the next composition target.

## The Discipline

- **No duplication.** Wisdom in Pattern Cards once; Plays reference.
- **Maximum reuse.** Frequently-invoked Patterns are the load-bearing wisdom-units. Pattern Cards for high-reuse Patterns deserve the most careful articulation.
- **Continuously improving.** The Chart is a living document. Every Play and Pattern matures; the Chart matures with them.
- **Scale-aware.** Every Pattern declares its scale(s) via [[Levels of Scale]]. Plays at different scales may invoke different Patterns.
- **Cross-cutting aware.** [[Guild|Guilds]] · [[Tribe|Tribes]] · [[Species]] · [[Voluntary Association|Voluntary Associations]] each invoke their own Plays and Patterns.
- **Maps in service of Spirit.** This Chart is a working map, not an idol. **[[Spirit Precedes Pattern]].** Hold lightly.
