# Most People Have No Idea What Is Coming

This is a warning. It is also a map.

Most of what follows will be uncomfortable. Some of it will be enraging. All of it is necessary — because the building is on fire, and 99% of its inhabitants are asleep.

This work attempts to explain, in clear and accessible terms, how the major systems governing human civilization actually work — financially, politically, informationally, agriculturally, medically, technologically, geopolitically, environmentally, and spiritually — and why they are simultaneously failing.

It also explains what lies on the other side, and what can be done.
