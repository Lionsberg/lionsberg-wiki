If you cannot [[Love]], at least do not [[Hate]].  

If you cannot [[Help]], at least do not [[Harm]]. 

If you cannot tell the [[Truth]], at least do not [[Deceive]]. 

If you cannot be [[Wise]], at least do not be Foolish.
