### 3. Belt Progression Is One Pattern, Verb-Not-Noun

Belts are **degrees of a unified coherent progression**, not separate Patterns and not categorical states. The verb-not-noun framing the nameless one articulated:

> _"Oh — we see that you are obviously blue-belting... congratulations... here is recognition."_

Belts are **recognized embodiments**, not awarded ranks. Among practitioners of the Way, belts are granted long after the observation of embodiment of that general level of consciousness and competence. The Pattern name will reflect this: **Belt Progression As Recognized Embodiment** (working name) — one Pattern, with the recognition discipline as part of its articulation.
