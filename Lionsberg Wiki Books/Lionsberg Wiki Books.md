# Lionsberg Wiki Books

The books below all explore the same core Challenges, Mission, Vision, and Goal, examining it from a variety of angles and possibilities. [[All]] centers around the [[ØNE]].

Each is a [[Work In Progress]]. All True ideas belong to [[The Commons]] and the body of thought, progress, and ideation to date. No ownership or originality is implied.

## The Sacred Codex — The Twelve Volumes

The canonical bound corpus. One work, in twelve movements, ordered accessible → structural → substrate. _Nothing materially excluded._

1. **Volume I** — [[The LIØNSBERG Experience]]  _(the Pilgrim's Path through the corpus)_
2. **Volume II** — [[The Story of LIØNSBERG]]  _(also woven throughout Volume IX)_
3. **Volume III** — [[The Great Game of LIØNSBERG]]
4. **Volume IV** — [[The LIØNSBERG Playbook]]
5. **Volume V** — [[The Kingdom of LIØNSBERG — Plans and Specifications]]  _(holds [[The LIØNSBERG Pattern Language|the Pattern Language]] as Part VI; nine-Part architecture)_
6. **Volume VI** — [[The LIØNSBERG Strategy and Plan]]  _(the 10 Year Grand Strategy, time-bound)_
7. **Volume VII** — [[The Program Delivery Guide]]  _(two forms preserved — original IPD/AEC + Earth in receivership)_
8. **Volume VIII** — [[The LIØNSBERG Lexicon]]  _(Meta Layer + 12 Pillars vocabulary)_
9. **Volume IX** — [[The Book of LIØNSBERG]]  _(carries The Story woven throughout)_
10. **Volume X** — [[The Sacred Ascent]]
11. **Volume XI** — [[The LIØNSBERG Living Library]]  _(curated index of the canonical body of work — the Codex as first entry, the Card-Book substrate, and substantive companion works)_
12. **Volume XII** — [[The LIØNSBERG Universe Guide]]  _(the Inheritance Manual; six Parts)_

The full canonical articulation, the Pilgrim's Path, the Canonical Discipline, the Voice Standard, and the Living Discipline live at [[The LIØNSBERG Sacred Codex|the Sacred Codex main page]].
