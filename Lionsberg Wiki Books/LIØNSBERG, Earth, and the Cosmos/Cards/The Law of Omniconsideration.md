# The Law of Omniconsideration

A foundational methodological law of the Codex.

**Every decision, every design, every articulation considers everything — every being, every scale, every consequence, every dimension, every time horizon, every relationship that might be touched.**

This is not paralysis. It is not perfectionism. It is the explicit refusal of the parochial, the short-term, the merely-human, the merely-local. It is the discipline by which the Pattern Language remains coherent across scales, the Story remains coherent across worlds, and the Game remains coherent across ages.

Omniconsideration is also the meta-method by which the **[[OmniSpection]]** review operates — the 360-degree seasonal evaluation by which Circles and the broader Body of LIØNSBERG detect drift, refine practice, and stay aligned with Creator's Intent.

The Law of Omniconsideration is closely paired with **The Law of Right Relationship** and **The Principle of Fractal Sovereignty** — together they form a triad: consider all, relate rightly, preserve sovereignty.

When in doubt, **consider more.** Consider what you have not yet considered. Consider whom you have not yet considered. Consider when you have not yet considered. The Pattern that holds across the most considerations is the truer Pattern.
