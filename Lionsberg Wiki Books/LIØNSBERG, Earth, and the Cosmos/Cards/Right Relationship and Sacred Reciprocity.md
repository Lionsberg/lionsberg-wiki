# Right Relationship and Sacred Reciprocity

The relational law of the New Civilization.

**Right Relationship** is the condition in which every being relates to every other being — and to land, water, life, and the Cosmos — in a way that allows All to flourish. It is the opposite of extraction. It is the opposite of domination. It is the opposite of the parasitic and the predatory.

**Sacred Reciprocity** is the feedback loop by which Right Relationship sustains itself: what is given returns; what is taken is repaid; the gift moves; the circuit closes. The Sacred Reciprocity Loop is the operational form of Right Relationship at scale.

These twin laws govern:

- How humans relate to one another (in Circles, Communities, Bioregions).
- How humans relate to the living world (land, water, ecosystems, animals).
- How humans relate to non-human intelligences (across species and dimensions).
- How beings relate to [[ØNE]] (the foundational reciprocity).

The 7:1 compressed ratio cap, the proof-of-contribution economy, the gift practices of healthy traditional cultures, the protocols of Cosmic Contact and Interspecies Diplomacy — all are operational instantiations of Right Relationship and Sacred Reciprocity.

Where these laws are kept, the New Civilization holds. Where these laws are broken, it falls back into kontrolle.
