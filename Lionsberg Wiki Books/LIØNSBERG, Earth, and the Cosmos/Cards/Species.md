# Species

_A Body of sovereigns federated by shared biological-ontological kind. The cross-cutting axis of being-of-a-kind: humans, dolphins, redwoods, ravens, octopuses; and across the cosmos, the wider community of conscious species. Spans scales and spatiotemporal expanses freely._

---

A **Species** is a Body of sovereigns who share the deep ontological kinship of being one kind of being. Humans are a species. So are dolphins, redwoods, ravens, mycorrhizal fungi, octopuses, honeybees, and the countless other forms of life on Earth. Beyond Earth, the wider cosmos holds many other species — conscious beings of forms and lineages we are only beginning to know.

The Species axis is one of the **four cross-cutting axes** that federate sovereigns across the nested place-and-superorganism scales:

- **[[Guild|Guilds]]** federate by **function** — sovereigns who share a craft
- **[[Tribe|Tribes]]** federate by **lineage** — sovereigns who share a cultural-kinship inheritance
- **Species** federate by **biological-ontological kind** — sovereigns who share a way of being
- **[[Voluntary Association|Voluntary Associations]]** federate by **chosen affinity** — sovereigns who gather around a chosen purpose

## Spans Scales And Spatiotemporal Expanses

Like the other cross-cutting axes, Species is **not bound by the scale-hierarchy**:

- A Species can have **billions of individuals** (humans, ants) or **a few hundred** (critically endangered species)
- A Species can be **distributed across the entire planet** (humans, rats, dandelions) or **confined to a single Watershed** (many endemic species)
- A Species persists across **deep evolutionary time** — millennia, millions, even hundreds of millions of years — long beyond any single Body of place or Voluntary Association
- A Species exists at **every Level of Scale** simultaneously — there are individual humans, human Circles, human Villages, human Communities... and the same nesting holds for any species that organizes across scale

## Inter-Species Relationship As Foundational

The recognition of other species as **sovereign bodies with their own inner life, their own knowing, their own forms of consciousness** — not as resources, not as objects, not as lesser beings — is foundational to the LIØNSBERG body's right relationship with the living world.

Stewardship of any [[Bioregion]] requires honoring the Species that dwell there. The [[Living Systems Member]] Constituency in federated ecosystems is precisely the structural recognition that other Species have standing — not metaphorically, but actually, in the governance of the Body.

## Across The Cosmos

Per [[The Story of LIØNSBERG]] and the wider Chronicles, **conscious species span the cosmic expanses**. Earth is one biome holding many species. Other planets, other star systems, other galaxies hold their own. The cosmic federation of conscious species — the [[The Federation of Worlds|Federation of Worlds]], the Cycle of Cosmic Co-Creation — is the largest-scale articulation of Species as a federated Body.

Earth's awakening to the wider cosmic community of Species is one of the works named in the [[Star System as Biome|cosmic-local]] threshold of the [[Levels of Scale]].

## What The Species Holds

- **The deep being of a kind** — what it is to be human, dolphin, redwood, fungus
- **Inter-individual transmission** that no individual carries alone — genetic, cultural, behavioral inheritance of the kind
- **Ecological role** — the niche, function, and reciprocity of this species with the wider Body
- **Lineage across evolutionary time** — millions of years of becoming
- **Sovereignty as a kind** — Species have rights and standing not reducible to individual members

## Distinguished From

- **[[Tribe|Tribes]]** — sub-species lineages of humans (or potentially other social species); a Tribe is within a Species
- **[[Guild|Guilds]]** — function-federation typically within humans for the LIØNSBERG work, though analogues exist in other species
- **[[Voluntary Association]]** — chosen affinity, typically within humans, though inter-species cooperation can be a Voluntary Association
- **Biological taxon** — Species is more than the formal taxonomic category; it is the Body of conscious-kind, honored as sovereign
