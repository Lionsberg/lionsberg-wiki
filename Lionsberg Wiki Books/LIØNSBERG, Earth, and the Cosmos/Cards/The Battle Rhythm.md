# The Battle Rhythm

The work-rest cadence operating at every fractal scale.

**Six and One. Six days of focused work. One day of Rest, Regeneration, and Retrospection.**

This is the operational rhythm of the New Civilization — the Sabbath pattern, kept across many traditions, restored as the foundational tempo of how Circles, Communities, and Federations actually operate.

The "six" of the cycle:
- Engaged, focused, productive co-creation
- Quests advanced, commitments kept
- The work of the week

The "one" of the cycle:
- **Rest** — bodies recover; nervous systems re-regulate
- **Regeneration** — energy stores replenish; relationships are tended; the land is honored
- **Retrospection** — what was learned? What did defects teach? What got better? What will be different next cycle?

The Battle Rhythm operates at every fractal scale:

- **Daily** — a period of focused work, a period of rest
- **Weekly** — six days, one Sabbath
- **Seasonal** — three months of quest, a fourth of rest and retrospection
- **Annual** — eleven months of work, one of jubilee
- **Septennial** — six years of labor, the seventh as Sabbatical year
- **Generational** — six generations of building, the seventh as Jubilee year — culminating in the Great Jubilee of [[Covenant and Jubilee — December 2033]]

The Battle Rhythm is **structural immunity against burnout**, against ossification, against drift, and against the Old World's relentless-production model that grinds beings down. The seventh day is not optional. It is what makes the other six sustainable across centuries.

It is also the rhythm of **integration** — what is learned in the doing is integrated in the resting. Without the rest, the learning never lands.
