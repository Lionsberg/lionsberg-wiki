# The LIØNSBERG Capacity Development Axis

The Sovereign Path has two axes. The **event axis** names what happens at each Stage — Discovery, Threshold, Orientation, Gather, and so on. The **capacity axis** names what is built in the Sovereign at each Stage. The events are the markers; the capacity is the substance.

| Stage | Event | Capacity Built |
|---|---|---|
| 1 · Discovery | Receive | Recognition — the felt knowing that this is real and is mine |
| 2 · Threshold | Awaken | Conscious choice — the Yes that aligns countless others |
| 3 · Orientation | Remember | Cosmic frame — context for what is happening, why now, what is at stake |
| 4 · Gather | Call | Identification — the felt knowing of who is ready among one's relations |
| 5 · First 90 Minutes | Begin | Circle-formation — the irreducible collective unit established |
| 6 · Weekly Practice | Run | Rhythm — Daily Way and Weekly Heartbeat sustained across seasons |
| 7 · Pattern Encounter | Encounter | Operational discernment — meeting Patterns as living answers, not theory |
| 8 · The 144 Gates | Initiate | Foundational competence — across body, heart, mind, spirit, field, voice, scale, story, service. Blue Belt in [[The Way Of The One Body]] by close. |
| 9 · The Sacred Ascent | Evolve | Deeper mastery — Black Belt in five archetypal Guild-Ways = 5th Dan in The Way Of The One Body; capacity for cosmic-scale conscious co-creation |
| 10 · Convergence | Federate | Multi-scale operation — holding Circle through Community through Bioregion through Planet simultaneously |
| 11 · Covenant and Jubilee | Renew | Full participation — when the last cohort earns Blue Belt; the species-wide Heaven embodied |
| 12 · Eternal Play | Co-Create | Lifelong participation in the Game without end — across generations, dimensions, worlds |

The Path is the cultivation of capacity. Each Stage names both what the Sovereign does and what the Sovereign becomes.
