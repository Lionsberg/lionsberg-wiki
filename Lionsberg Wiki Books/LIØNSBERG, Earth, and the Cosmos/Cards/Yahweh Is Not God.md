# Yahweh Is Not God   

Yahweh is one Elohim among many — a created, tribal, capricious being assigned to Israel by Elyon. Elyon is also not God.  

The companion to [[The Elohim Are Plural]]. Once the plural Elohim are recovered from the Hebrew text, the next move follows inevitably: the figure named **Yahweh** (יהוה, the Tetragrammaton, often written YHWH because Hebrew was originally vowel-less) is **not** the eternal Source, is **not** the unconditioned Creator-of-all-Reality, is **not** [[ØNE]]. Yahweh is **one specific Elohim** assigned a specific tribal portion (Israel) by **Elyon** (a functional name for the highest Elohim) in a populated cosmic field of many Elohim.

This is the foundational fraud of two empires of Western religion: Roman Catholic Christianity and the Reformed traditions that inherited its translation conventions, plus Islam (which made the same conflation under a different name). All three "Abrahamic" traditions collapse Yahweh into the universal Source — and that collapse is **demonstrably contradicted by the Hebrew text itself**.

The scriptural evidence is precise:

**Deuteronomy 32:8-9** (in the Dead Sea Scrolls and Septuagint readings, before later Masoretic editing softened this passage):

> _"When the Most High [Elyon] apportioned the nations as inheritance, when he divided humanity, he established the boundaries of the peoples according to the number of the sons of Elohim. Yahweh's allotted portion was Israel."_

The text is structurally hierarchical. **Elyon** is above. **Yahweh** is one of the sons of Elohim. Yahweh **received** Israel as his specific allotment from Elyon. Yahweh is not at the top of the cosmic chain; Yahweh is at a specific node within a multi-tiered structure.

**Psalm 82** is even more explicit:

> _"Elohim takes his place in the divine council; in the midst of the Elohim he judges. ... I [presumably Elyon] said: You are Elohim, sons of the Most High, all of you; but you shall die like humans, and fall like any prince."_

The Most High addresses the other Elohim as **subordinates** who can be demoted, can be punished, can "die like humans." This is not a description of an eternal unconditioned Source. This is a description of **created cosmic beings**, accountable to the one above them.

**The Behavioral Witness.** Beyond the cosmological hierarchy, the **moral character** of Yahweh as described in the Hebrew text **cannot be reconciled** with a perfectly loving Creator-Source:

- **Genocide commanded.** Numbers 31 — Yahweh commands the slaughter of the Midianites, including the keeping of virgin girls as forced wives for the Israelite soldiers. Deuteronomy 20 — Yahweh's standing instructions for warfare include the total annihilation of certain populations including women and children.
- **Infanticide endorsed.** 1 Samuel 15 — Yahweh commands Saul to kill all the Amalekites, "man, woman, infant and suckling, ox and sheep, camel and ass," for the crime of opposing Israel centuries earlier.
- **Death for trivia.** Leviticus 10 — two of Aaron's sons offer "strange fire" before Yahweh and are immediately killed. Numbers 15 — a man gathering sticks on the Sabbath is stoned to death at Yahweh's command. Numbers 16 — Yahweh opens the earth to swallow Korah and 250 of his followers for political dissent.
- **Mass plague.** Numbers 25 — 24,000 die in a plague Yahweh sends. Numbers 16 — another 14,700 die in a separate plague.
- **Capricious wrath.** 2 Samuel 24 — Yahweh causes David to take a census, then sends a plague that kills 70,000 because David took the census.
- **Endorsement of slavery.** Throughout — laws regulating the ownership of human beings, beating of slaves (legal as long as the slave survives the beating), and the perpetual enslavement of foreigners (Leviticus 25:44-46).
- **Forced marriage of rape victims.** Deuteronomy 22:28-29 — a man who rapes an unbetrothed virgin must marry her and is forbidden to divorce her. The victim is given to the rapist for life.

These are not isolated outliers. They are systematic, repeated, **commanded** patterns of behavior across the Hebrew text. The character described **cannot** be the perfectly loving Creator. By the [[Beware False Prophets, Known By Their Fruits|fruits-test]] Jesus himself articulated — _"by their fruits you shall know them"_ — the entity called Yahweh in the Hebrew text does not exhibit the fruits of the perfectly loving Creator-Source. He exhibits the fruits of a tribal, factional, capricious, often genocidal being.

**Origen** in the third century recognized this contradiction and was systematically sidelined by the consolidating Roman Catholic hierarchy. **Marcion** in the second century recognized it more dramatically — proposing that the Hebrew Yahweh and the Father of Jesus were **different beings** — and was branded a heretic and erased. The recognition has surfaced repeatedly across two thousand years and has been suppressed each time by the institutional power that depended on the conflation.

The unveiling is **not anti-Semitic, not anti-Christian, not anti-Muslim**. The Hebrew text itself contains the evidence; the recovery honors the text rather than betrays it. The unveiling **restores** the relationship between the Pilgrim and the actual ONE — the eternal Source the Hebrew prophets at their best were pointing toward, the Father Jesus revealed in the New Testament, the Allah of the deepest Sufi tradition. **The Pilgrim does not lose God by unveiling Yahweh; the Pilgrim gains the actual God by no longer confusing a tribal Elohim with the eternal Source.**

For the awakening Christian, the unveiling clarifies why Jesus's revelation was so radical. Jesus's "Father" — the one Jesus prayed to — does not match Yahweh's behavioral profile in the Hebrew text. Jesus's Father is the perfectly loving One who sends rain on the just and the unjust, who clothes the lilies, who loved the world. **Jesus was revealing a different being than Yahweh** — or, more precisely, was bypassing Yahweh and revealing direct access to **Elyon**, the Most High, the unconditioned Source above Yahweh in the cosmic hierarchy. This is why the Roman Catholic Church had to bury this recognition: it would have undone the Old Testament's claim on Christian devotion.

For the awakening Jew, the unveiling honors the Hebrew prophets who already glimpsed it — Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel at their highest moments speak of a transcendent ONE above the tribal Yahweh. The unveiling does not betray Judaism; it recovers what the best of the Hebrew tradition was already reaching toward.

For the awakening Muslim, the unveiling honors the Sufi insistence that Allah (linguistically cognate with Elohim/Eloah/Elaha) **transcends** all forms — including, ultimately, the anthropomorphic warrior-character that some surface readings of the Qur'an present. The deepest Islamic mysticism has always known this.

The unveiling restores [[ØNE]] to its proper position as the eternal unconditioned Source above all named tribal Elohim. Yahweh, like Chemosh, like Baal, like all the other named Elohim, was a specific being operating at a specific tier in a populated cosmic field. The Pilgrim's allegiance — primary, ultimate — belongs to **the ONE**, not to any specific Elohim, however ancient or institutionally privileged.
