# The Weekly Heartbeat
_The rhythm beneath every Living Circle_

---

## What This Is

The Weekly Heartbeat is the recurring weekly gathering of a Sovereign Circle. The same day. The same hour. The same practice. Returning each week.

It is the Rhythm a Circle cannot live without — and the one most often skipped, with the most predictable consequence.

**Circles that hold the Heartbeat live. Circles that drop the Heartbeat dissolve.**

---

## Why It Matters

A Circle is a living Body composed of sovereign cells. Like any Body, it stays alive through rhythm — breath, heartbeat, sleep, season. The Weekly Heartbeat is the minimum-viable Body rhythm at the Circle scale.

When the Heartbeat is dropped, the cells lose contact. Trust evaporates in days. The Quest drifts. The Circle dies quietly — no breach, no drama, just dissolution.

When the Heartbeat is held faithfully, the cells stay woven. Trust deepens even in weeks when little visible work happens. The Quest moves forward, week by week. The Circle survives the first season, the first year, the long Walk.

---

## How To Set It

See **[[Set Your Cadence]]** for the canonical Play.

The basic shape:

- **The same day each week.** Predictability is the gift.
- **The same hour.** Body rhythm depends on it.
- **The same place** — or the same call. The room becomes part of the practice.
- **A minimum of one hour.** Many Circles run ninety minutes; some run two.
- **Honored above other commitments.** When the Heartbeat is moved, the Body fibrillates.

The cadence is a Circle decision, not an individual one. Once decided, it is held by all.

---

## The Basic Structure

Most Living Circles hold the Heartbeat in four movements:

1. **Open.** A brief ritual that crosses the threshold from the world's time into the Circle's time. Silence, breath, intention, a reading, a check-in — whatever marks the entry.
2. **Share.** Each Sovereign speaks briefly to where they are. What is alive, what is heavy, what is being learned.
3. **Work.** The substantive time. The Quest moves forward. Decisions are made. Commitments are tracked. The next week is named.
4. **Close.** A brief ritual that crosses back out. Gratitude, confirmation of the next gathering, a closing word or breath.

Some Circles add a fifth movement — **Eat** — and the meal becomes part of the Heartbeat. This often deepens the work.

## The Heartbeat At Every Scale

The Weekly Heartbeat is the Circle-scale Rhythm. The same pattern repeats at every Fractal Scale:

- **Sovereign daily** — [[The Daily Way]] and [[The Coherent Divine Intention Wave]]
- **Circle weekly** — The Weekly Heartbeat (this page)
- **Guild monthly or seasonal** — gatherings of practitioners across geography
- **Community monthly or seasonal** — gatherings of the wider local body
- **Bioregion seasonal** — equinox and solstice convergences (see [[The LIØNSBERG Calendar]])
- **Planet annual and septennial** — covenant moments and the larger turnings
- **Cosmos generational and beyond** — the Eternal Play breathing across ages

Every scale has its Heartbeat. The Circle Heartbeat is the one the others rest on — without it, the larger Rhythms have nothing to gather around.
