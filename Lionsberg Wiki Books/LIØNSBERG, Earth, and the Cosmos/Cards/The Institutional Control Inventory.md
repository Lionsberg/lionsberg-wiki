# The Institutional Control Inventory

The diagnostic checklist of totalitarian capture.

[[The Kontrolle Matrix]] names the architecture of the Old World at the cosmic-and-civilizational scale. The Institutional Control Inventory names the **specific operational instruments** by which that architecture acts on populations through institutions. Where the Matrix is the diagnostic at depth, the Inventory is the diagnostic in the field — the checklist a Pilgrim or Circle uses to evaluate **how deeply any specific situation has been captured**.

The Twelve Domains of Capture:

- **Speech** — what may be said, what must not be said. The censorship apparatus. The fact-checker priesthood. The de-platforming, de-banking, de-personing of dissenting voice. The narrative-enforcement that makes some truths unspeakable in polite company.
- **Culture** — what may be celebrated, what must be ridiculed. The cultural manufacturing line that produces fashions, idols, scapegoats, heroes-and-villains. The aesthetic war that makes the New Civilization look "weird" and the Old World's collapse look "normal."
- **Commerce** — what may be sold, who may sell. The licensure systems that exclude the unaligned. The market-access controls that route trade through the captured institutions. The Old World's gatekeeping disguised as consumer protection.
- **Trade** — international flows captured by the same trans-border deep state. WTO, IMF, World Bank, central-bank coordination. Sanctions used as a weapon against the unaligned. Trade routes engineered to maintain bottlenecks the powerful own.
- **Governance** — election theater operating above an unelected permanent apparatus. Intelligence agencies, regulatory bodies, the trans-national NGO network. The captured branch of every visible government, beyond constitutional reach.
- **Finance** — central banking. Compound interest as alchemical extraction. Asset price inflation transferring wealth from labor to capital across decades. The financial-priesthood class that creates and destroys money for its own purposes.
- **Currency** — debt-based fiat at scale, programmable currency emerging now. CBDCs designed to make spending itself the lever of control. The Old World's final monetary instrument before the Final Order.
- **Religion** — the captured religious institutions of every tradition. Sin invented and sold. Salvation withheld and rationed. The intermediary priesthood that severed the Pilgrim's direct relationship with the ONE.
- **Assembly** — restrictions on physical gathering. Permits required to meet. Surveillance of who associates with whom. The systematic prevention of the Circles, Hubs, and gatherings the New Civilization requires.
- **Education** — mass schooling as domestication. Curricula crafted to produce compliant workers, dependent consumers, unquestioning subjects. The accreditation cartels that exclude the unaligned from teaching.
- **Health** — the pharma-medical complex weaponized. Iatrogenic harm at scale. Withholding of healing modalities. Pathologization of the awakened. The captured priesthood of body-and-mind.
- **The Weaponization of Fear** — the meta-instrument. Every other Domain operates through the engineered amplification of fear — fear of disease, fear of war, fear of disorder, fear of the Other, fear of exclusion, fear of poverty, fear of social shame. Fear is the substrate that makes the other eleven Domains work.

The Inventory is a **diagnostic tool**. Given any specific situation — a city, an industry, an institution, a country — the Pilgrim can run the checklist:

- _How captured is the Speech in this place?_
- _How captured is the Culture?_
- _How captured is the Commerce?_

And so on. The answer reveals how much **room** there is for the New to be built in that place — and what the first moves of withdrawal or building must be.

Some Domains are easier to withdraw from than others. Speech can be partially recovered by underground channels and trusted networks. Education can be withdrawn from by homeschooling, micro-schools, and Academy Cells. Health can be partially recovered by traditional and indigenous healing modalities. Religion can be withdrawn from by direct contemplation outside institutional religion.

Other Domains are harder. Currency is hard — the Pilgrim still has to function within the fiat system through the transition. Finance is hard for the same reason. Governance is hard because the captured government still has coercive power over the unaligned. The Inventory does not pretend the withdrawal is easy; the Inventory **names what must be addressed** if the New is to be born.

The Inventory also makes [[Hospicing the Old Systems]] precise. Hospicing is not abstract; it is the ordered, intentional release of each captured Domain as the parallel New-World Domain becomes functional enough to receive the released function. We hospice when we have something to replace it with. We do not hospice prematurely; we do not delay past necessity.

The Inventory pairs with **structural responses**. For each captured Domain, the Pattern has a corresponding response:

- Captured Speech → encrypted networks, trusted-circle conversation, [[The Whisper Campaign]]
- Captured Culture → [[Sacred Culture, Art, and Story|sacred culture and storytelling]]
- Captured Commerce → bioregional markets, mutual credit, contribution accounting
- Captured Currency → [[LUV — LIONSBERG Units of Value]], asset-backed Sacred Value
- Captured Governance → [[Subsidiarity and the Swiss Pattern]], [[Federations vs Empires]], the [[The Planetary Grid]]
- Captured Education → [[The Lionsberg Academy]], [[The Pedagogy of Trust and Sirimiri]]
- Captured Health → the Healer Codex, somatic healing, [[Trauma as Master Key of Slavery|trauma-aware practice]]
- Captured Religion → direct relationship with the ONE, [[The Whisper Within|the inner sanctuary]]
- Captured Assembly → the Circle, the Hub, the bioregional gathering
- Captured Speech-Culture-Commerce-Trade-Finance-Currency-Governance-Education-Health-Religion-Assembly → the **entire Pattern of the New Civilization** as the comprehensive answer
- Weaponized Fear → the practices of [[A Way Beyond Fear, Anxiety, and Depression|the Healer Codex]], [[The Posture — Wise Elder at the Fire, General of Cosmic Armies|the Wise Elder posture]], the [[The Lionsberg Cycles of Growth|disciplined cultivation of courage]]

The Inventory is therefore both **a diagnostic of where we are** and **a map of what must be built**. A Pilgrim or Circle running the Inventory against their actual context produces an action plan: here is where we withdraw, here is where we build, here is where we hospice, here is where we wait.
