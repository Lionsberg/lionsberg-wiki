# One of the Ones of the ONE

The signature LIØNSBERG identity formula.

**Each of the All are One of the Ones of the ONE.**

Each being is:

- **One** — a discrete, sovereign expression.
- **Of the Ones** — among the family of discrete sovereign expressions.
- **Of the ONE** — within, of, permeated by, and sustained by the [[ONE]].

The formula collapses the false binary between individual and collective. A being is **simultaneously**:

- A unique, unrepeatable expression with its own Reality
- A member of the family of beings
- A localization of the ONE itself, not separate from it

This is not pantheism (the ONE _equals_ the All).
It is not monotheism-with-creatures (the ONE _made_ the All but is separate from them).
It is the actual ontological grammar: **the ONE creatively experiences ItSelf as the All, each of which retains discrete sovereignty and yet remains of and within ONE.**

Every being can say, accurately and completely:

> _I am one. I am one of the Ones. I am of the ONE._

This formula belongs in [[The LIØNSBERG Lexicon]] (Volume V) as a foundational identity entry, and reverberates through Volume I (the Story), Volume II (the Pattern of [[Fractal Sovereignty]]), and Volume VII (the Sacred Ascent's union-without-dissolution).
