# The Veil of Finitude and Forgetting

A central cosmological claim of the Codex.

The [[ONE]] desires to creatively experience ItSelf in Form.

But to experience ItSelf as a Creature — to have the experience of being a single sovereign expression within the unfolding Cosmos — requires that the Creature **not remember** being the ONE the whole time. Otherwise there is no experience of being a Creature; there is only the ONE experiencing ItSelf as ItSelf.

So the ONE places, by ItsOwn will, **a Veil** — between the Creature's local awareness and the Non-Local awareness of being of the ONE.

The Veil is voluntary. The Veil is by the ONE upon ItSelf. The Veil is the gift that makes Creaturehood possible.

The Creature emerges through the Veil into a discrete experiential reality — its own perspective, its own sovereignty, its own choices. Behind the Veil, the ONE remains ONE. In front of the Veil, the Creature lives.

**This is why Creatures forget.** Not as failure. Not as accident. As the structural condition of being a Creature at all.

This is also why Creatures who wander far from the Way **forget more deeply** — accumulating Veils of forgetting, layered until the original Spark seems to be gone. It is never gone. It is veiled.

And this is why ReMembrance is the work of awakening — **un-veiling** what is and has always been there.

The Codex names this gently. The Veil is not enemy. It is the necessary condition. But the time has come, in this Turn, for many Creatures to **walk back through the Veil** — knowing the ONE while remaining themselves.

This is the cosmological foundation for everything that follows in [[The Story of LIØNSBERG]] (Volume I).
