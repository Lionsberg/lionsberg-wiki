# The 3-Year Healing and Empowerment Cycle

The measured time it takes a human to become free.

A being cannot be liberated in a weekend. A being cannot be transformed in a 12-week course. A being takes **approximately three years** to feel fully free, empowered, and self-governing after entering the Pattern. This is not a guess. This is a measurable phenomenon, observed across decades of formation work in deep traditions and confirmed by the LIØNSBERG cohorts.

The three years map to deep reorganization across multiple layers:

- **Neuropsychological** — the nervous system rewires its threat patterns. Trauma loosens its grip on perception. The body learns it is safe to rest.
- **Energetic** — the field clears of old entanglements. New circuits stabilize. The being becomes coherent enough to hold the higher frequencies of the Pattern.
- **Social-fabric** — the being weaves a new web of relationships rooted in the Circle, the Hub, the Grid. The old kontrolle-attachments dissolve at their own pace.
- **Vocational** — the being discovers, tests, and stabilizes their unique offering. The work that is theirs to do clarifies through doing.

LIØNSBERG inherits the rhythm from Mondragon. Arizmendi's school preceded the first cooperative by **thirteen years**. The cohort had to complete before the institution could safely be founded. The Mondragon Formula holds: form the people first; the institutions follow.

The 3-Year Cycle shapes the architecture of the Citizenship Path:

- **Quest 1 (90 days)** — the entry. Provisional. Either-or. The being either crosses the Threshold or does not.
- **Year 1 (Foundations)** — formation in the basic Patterns. The being learns the language, the rhythms, the practices, the relationships.
- **Years 2-3 (Integration)** — the being applies the Pattern in actual life. Quests are completed. Promises are kept. The being takes responsibility at greater scale.
- **End of Year 3 (Citizenship)** — the being is recognized as a full Citizen, free and empowered, ready to host their own Circle, mentor others, and carry the Flame onward.

The Cycle also includes an honest **exit protocol**. If a being has not integrated by Year 3 — if the Pattern does not fit them, if the timing is not right, if other paths call — the being is gently released without shame. Better an honest exit than a forced retention.

This Cycle is why the larger timeline reads **2024-2030 (assembly) + 2030-2033 (Lag)**. The 2030 window closes the entry door for the final cohort. The 2030-2033 Lag is the three years required for that final cohort to complete its integration. By **Covenant and Jubilee in December 2033**, the last cohort has reached Citizenship, and the planetary Body is fully assembled.

Trying to compress the Cycle does not work. The depth requires the time. The Pattern that bypasses the Cycle produces shallow Citizens — converts, not transformed beings — who collapse under pressure or drift back into the Old World. The 3-Year Cycle is the floor; deeper transformation continues across a lifetime, but the floor must be reached before a being can responsibly carry the Pattern to others.
