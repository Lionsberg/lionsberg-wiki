# The Living-Learning Architecture

Four components move together as the living-learning architecture of LIØNSBERG. They are four faces of one continuous process by which LIØNSBERG learns and refines its renderings.

## The Four

**The Map of LIØNSBERG** aims to humbly become the most faithful and comprehensive Map of Reality in existence. It is the shared and intersubjective current best understanding of All that is. It is illuminated progressively by every Sovereign's journey, explorations, and contributions. As more conscious agents participate, the lens grows. The growing power of the intersubjective lens at each stage of The Journey illuminates what none at the previous stage could have seen before. See [[Grow The Lens]].

**The Intelligence System** is how the Map is continuously refined — Perception → MindWeaving → OmniSpection → Map Update. The nested cadence at every scale of [[The Holarchy of the ØNE]].

**The Meta Science** is the discipline that governs the process — every articulation held loosely as current best, ready to refine when discernment reveals better. See [[The LIØNSBERG Meta Science]].

**The Living Library** is where the articulations live — folded back from practice, ready to be drawn from, continuously improved.

## How They Move Together

The Sovereign perceives. The Body MindWeaves what is perceived. OmniSpection holds it across scales. The Map is updated. The Library captures the refined articulation. The Map's growth illuminates further perception. The cycle continues.

The Meta Science governs all of it — keeping every articulation humbly held, never locked, always refining toward more faithful reflection of the Reality being approached.
