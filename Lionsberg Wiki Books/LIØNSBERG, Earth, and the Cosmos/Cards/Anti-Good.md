# Anti-Good

The negative ethical axis of the Codex.

**Goodness** is that which Loves and Helps Others rise toward [[The Goal]].

**Anti-Goodness** — **Anti-Good** — is the conscious infliction of Fear and Harm. It is the active opposite of Goodness, not merely its absence.

The distinction between "not good" and "Anti-Good" matters:

- **Not good** includes: ignorance, weakness, failure, incompleteness, the not-yet-mature. These are ordinary conditions of Creaturehood. Compassion is the right response. Growth is the right movement.
- **Anti-Good** is: the deliberate infliction of suffering, the calculated cultivation of separation, the engineered theft of sovereignty, the conscious feeding of the Dark. Compassion alone is insufficient response. [[Sacred Guardianship — Neutralizing the Predatory|Sacred Guardianship]] is required.

The Codex does not pretend Anti-Good does not exist. To pretend it does not exist is to leave the vulnerable unprotected and the predatory unchecked. To name it accurately is to enable the structural immunity that protects what is being co-created.

**Anti-Good is also a recognizable Pattern.** Like the Pattern of Good, the Anti-Good Pattern has structural features that repeat across scales and species. It produces Empires, it weaponizes religions, it captures institutions, it feeds on trauma, it harvests life-force. It has always done this. Recognizing the Pattern is what allows it to be named and neutralized wherever it appears.

The Codex does not give Anti-Good more attention than it deserves. The orientation is toward The Goal. But it does name Anti-Good clearly, so the New Civilization is not built atop unrecognized predation.

Lives canonically in [[The LIØNSBERG Lexicon]] as the explicit negative axis. Foundational to [[The InterCosmic Treaty of Non-Subjugation]], [[Sacred Guardianship — Neutralizing the Predatory]], and the moral discernment of Volume V.
