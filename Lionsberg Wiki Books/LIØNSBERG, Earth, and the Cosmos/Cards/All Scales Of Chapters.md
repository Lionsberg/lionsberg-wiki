# All Scales Of Chapters

[[The Story of LIØNSBERG]] plays at every scale, holofractally. The same Story is breathed at different breath at every level of [[The Holarchy of the ØNE]].

## The Scales

- **The Cosmic Chapter** — LIØNSBERG before this world, across worlds, since the Eternal Citadel was kindled
- **Earth's Chapter** — the current world-rendering of LIØNSBERG
- **Bioregional Chapters** — the local-land renderings
- **Local Chapters** — community-scale renderings
- **Circle Chapters** — what the Circle weaves together
- **Individual Chapters** — each Sovereign's life as a Chapter

## How The Chapters Compose

[[Share Your Stories]] is the practice by which each life becomes a Chapter; each Chapter becomes part of The Story; The Story becomes the living tapestry across all scales. Each Chapter comes alive as a Chapter of The Story of LIØNSBERG when consciously articulated.

The Story is a Living Book. The spine is what The Story sets down; the weaving is what the Sovereigns add. The Book is never finished. The closer one walks, the more one becomes its author.
