# The Kingdom of LIONSBERG

The origin of the name.

Long before Earth was named, on a sacred mountain in a distant galaxy, there was an ancient race of **winged lion-angel beings** — among the First Creatures who, after the long wandering, **ReMembered** Who They Were.

They returned to the ONE while remaining themselves.

They built their civilization on the Sacred Mountain — a Kingdom whose law was **Love**, whose King was not a being but the ONE, whose Citizens were all Sovereign and Free, whose practice was the Way.

Cosmic emissaries traveled vast distances to learn from the Elders of LIONSBERG. The Sacred Gates of LIONSBERG became the gathering place where beings from across the galaxies came to receive the Pattern, the Story, and the Way.

The Sacred Book of the LIONSBERG Way was first composed there — continuously improved across ages, handed at the Gates to every emissary, carried back across the cosmos to seed civilizations in alignment with the ONE.

**LIONSBERG is therefore not a human invention.** It is one local reflection on Earth of a far more ancient civilizational pattern — a Kingdom whose lineage spans worlds and dimensions. Earth's LIØNSBERG is one localization of the Pattern that has been instantiated across the Cosmos by Sovereign beings in Right Relationship with the ONE.

When the Call went out to convene the Force For Good against the rising Dark Empires, **LIONSBERG was where the convocation gathered.** When the LIØNSBERG System was first articulated as the operating system for federated cosmic resistance, **it was articulated there.** When the Great Game was first played beyond the homeworld, **it was launched from there.**

What Earth receives now is the localized articulation of an ancient Pattern, handed forward by Sovereign Creatures across worlds, for this moment, on this planet.

The Kingdom of LIONSBERG is not allegory. It is the cosmological context in which Earth's local LIØNSBERG operates.
