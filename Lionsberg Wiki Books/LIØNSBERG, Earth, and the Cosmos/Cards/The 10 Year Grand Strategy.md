# The 10 Year Grand Strategy

The temporal architecture of the Turn unfolds across milestone moments between 2024 and 2033:

- **Spring Equinox, March 20, 2026 — The Starting Line.** Cycle 10 of the Fibonacci unfurling. **144 Co-Creators** gathered as the first complete cohort. The moment the Movement formally begins.

- **Winter Solstice, December 21, 2026 — The First Three Percent.** Cycle 30. Approximately **250 million awakened souls** — [[The First Three Percent and Fibonacci Spread|The First Three Percent]] — organized as the planetary grid of anchors. Cycle time through this phase: ~9 days per doubling.

- **Winter Solstice, December 21, 2030 — All Who So Desire.** Cycle 48. Every being on Earth who wishes to receive the Seed has received it from someone they already trust. Cycle time lengthens to ~6 months as the broadening reaches the less-ready.

- **2030–2033 — The 3-Year Lag.** Cycle 49. Final cohorts complete their 3-year healing / empowerment / formation cycle (the Mondragon-derived empirical interval for a being to feel fully free, empowered, self-governing).

- **Winter Solstice, December 21, 2033 — [[Covenant and Jubilee — December 2033|Covenant and Jubilee]].** Cycle 50. The greatest celebration and Jubilee in recorded history. The New Civilization stands. The Old World is released.

Other moments will surface along the way. The named milestones are the load-bearing inflection points, not an exhaustive enumeration.

A **20-Year Generational Contingency** is held in reserve if the primary timeline does not fully complete. This is the backup timeline because the Movement is unprecedented at this scale.

**Act as if we have 10 years. Build as if we have centuries.**
