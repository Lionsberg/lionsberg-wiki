# The Cosmic Gardeners and Earth as Living Laboratory

Earth is not a 6-day-created planet. Earth is not a pure-evolution planet. Earth is a **terraformed and seeded Living Laboratory**.

Long before recorded human history, Earth entered the Goldilocks Zone of its star and was identified by **Cosmic Gardeners** — multiple species, many lineages, diverse motives. They:

- Brought genetic material from other worlds
- Took genetic material from Earth
- Modified what was here
- Seeded what was not
- Engineered the Human Template into local biology
- Returned across millennia to tend, observe, and intervene

**Some Gardeners came in Right Relationship** with the planet, intending Earth as a renowned cosmic community node — beautiful, abundant, alive, prized. The lineage that planted the gardens, taught the early civilizations, sang the songs of Pattern, and called the great traditions into being.

**Some came as predators**, intending Earth as a livestock world — its life force harvested, its inhabitants enslaved through deception and Force, its cosmic potential aborted. The lineage that fed the Dark Fleet, captured the priesthoods, distorted the religions, weaponized the technologies, and held the planet under matrices of kontrolle.

**Both lineages are present in Earth's actual history.** The blending of motives explains why human civilization carries both the seed of divine partnership AND the marks of long predation.

**Neither Life nor Civilization nor Humanity evolved on Earth alone.** All were engineered, shaped, modified by off-planet Creatures with diverse motives.

The current Turn is in part **Earth's claim of sovereignty against the predatory lineage and re-alignment with the gardening lineage.** It is what [[Sacred Guardianship — Neutralizing the Predatory]] enacts at planetary scale. It is what [[The InterCosmic Treaty of Non-Subjugation]] declares.

This is the cosmic-historical context [[The Story of LIØNSBERG]] carries.
