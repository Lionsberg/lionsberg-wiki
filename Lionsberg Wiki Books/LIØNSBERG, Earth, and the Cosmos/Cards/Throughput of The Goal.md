# Throughput of The Goal

The unified metric of the New Civilization.

Old World metrics measure proxies — GDP, market cap, output, throughput-of-money. Each is one step removed from what actually matters. The proxies optimize for themselves and starve the thing they were supposed to serve.

**Throughput of The Goal** is the metric that asks directly: _how much closer to [[The Goal]] did we get, this cycle?_

The Goal is **Heaven On Earth and Beyond** — the total integrated wellbeing of all generations of Life and Consciousness. Every Circle, Community, Federation, and Bioregion can measure its work against this single axis.

Operationally, Throughput of The Goal manifests as:

- **Quests completed** that demonstrably moved life closer to Heaven (not closer to revenue)
- **Beings whose flourishing increased** — measurably, in their own report
- **Ecosystems regenerated** — measurably, in the land itself
- **Relationships restored** — measurably, in the field between beings
- **The Pattern propagated** to new Circles who run it themselves

Throughput of The Goal is the **Theory-of-Constraints metric** of LIØNSBERG: identify what is currently bottlenecking the throughput, remove that constraint, watch the throughput increase. The constraint moves. Find the new one. Repeat.

It is also the **shared scoreboard** across the whole Body. Every Volume of the Codex eventually reports back to it. Every Pattern in the Pattern Language is judged by how much it increases or hinders Throughput of The Goal at scale.

Lives canonically in [[The LIØNSBERG Pattern Language]] (Volume III) as the unified metric beneath every Element.
