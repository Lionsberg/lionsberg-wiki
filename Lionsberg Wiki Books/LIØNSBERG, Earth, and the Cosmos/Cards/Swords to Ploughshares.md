# Swords to Ploughshares

The financing thesis.

The world spends an enormous portion of its resources on military, on weapons, on Force. The Lionsberg Strategy and Plan of Action articulates a **phased redirection** of these resources, year over year, into the infrastructure of life.

The arithmetic, per the Strategy document:

- Baseline global military spending across a ten-year horizon: approximately **$30 trillion**
- Phased reallocation of approximately **10% per year** redirected from weapons production to **livingry** (the Buckminster Fuller term for tools-of-life)
- Cumulative reallocation over the window: approximately **$15-20 trillion** freed for [[The Hub Network Topology]], the regenerative industries, the food and water sovereignty systems, the education systems, the healing systems
- Residual military budget: dropping toward approximately $13 trillion over the decade — enough for sovereign defense within [[The InterCosmic Treaty of Non-Subjugation]], not enough for empire

The thesis is **not pacifism**. The Sacred Warrior remains. Sovereign defense is honored. But the proportion shifts radically — from a planet armed for endless war to a planet structured for endless flourishing.

The mechanism is **consent-and-redirection**, not seizure. As [[Hospicing the Old Systems|the Old Systems hospice]], the resources they had captured become available for the New. Beings withdraw from weapons-funded employment into livingry-funded employment. Capital reallocates as the Pattern becomes obviously the wise right thing.

The phrase "Swords to Ploughshares" is from the prophet Isaiah, deeply rooted in the cosmic register the Codex speaks in. The reallocation is the operational fulfillment of that ancient prophecy at planetary scale.

**$15-20 trillion is approximately what it costs to build the Hub Network at full scale.** The reallocation and the construction are the same act, completed across the same window.
