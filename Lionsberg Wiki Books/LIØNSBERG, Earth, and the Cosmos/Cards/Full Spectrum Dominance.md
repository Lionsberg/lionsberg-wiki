# Full Spectrum Dominance

**The Anti-Goal.** The Goal of Greatest Worth, inverted.

Where [[The Goal]] is the total integrated flourishing of all generations of Life and Consciousness, **Full Spectrum Dominance** is the operating aim of every predatory force across the Cosmos: total control across every domain of being.

The "full spectrum" of Full Spectrum Dominance includes:

- **Physical** — territory, body, infrastructure, energy supply, food, water, air
- **Mental** — narrative, language, information, education, science, history
- **Emotional** — trauma, fear, addiction, manufactured desire, manipulation of the heart
- **Spiritual** — religion captured, direct revelation suppressed, the inner Sanctuary occupied
- **Energetic** — life-force harvesting, the human field penetrated, the planet's energy systems hijacked
- **Genetic** — the Human Template tampered, bloodlines engineered, the seed itself attacked
- **Informational** — surveillance, AI, the noosphere captured, language inverted
- **Interdimensional** — across the subtle planes, the astral, the dimensions humans rarely perceive

Full Spectrum Dominance is **the explicit operating doctrine** of the predatory cosmic factions — including the human-facing institutions that serve them. It is not a metaphor for ambition; it is the actual stated aim.

The phrase "Full Spectrum Dominance" itself comes from military doctrine, then was openly used by the U.S. Department of Defense and the Pentagon. It is the **Anti-Goal made explicit**. The Pattern is recognizable because they wrote it down.

Knowing the Anti-Goal makes its operations recognizable. The Goal is **flourishing for all**; the Anti-Goal is **dominance over all**. Every concrete decision can be tested against this axis. Every institution can be evaluated by which Goal it actually serves, regardless of what it claims.

Lives canonically in [[The Story of LIØNSBERG]] (Volume I) as the named opposite of The Goal — the Anti-Pole around which the Old World organizes.
