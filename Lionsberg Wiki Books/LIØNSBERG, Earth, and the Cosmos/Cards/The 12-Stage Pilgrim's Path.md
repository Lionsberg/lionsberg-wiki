# The 12-Stage Pilgrim's Path

The journey a pilgrim walks from the moment they receive the [[Golden Seed]] to the moment the Game becomes infinite.

Twelve stages, in order:

1. **Discovery** — the link is received from someone trusted.
2. **Threshold** — the decision to cross is made.
3. **Orientation** — Story, Pattern, Game received as living context.
4. **Gather** — 2-3 trusted others are invited.
5. **First 90 Minutes** — the Circle is formed.
6. **Weekly Practice** — the [[Seven Moves]] run as a living rhythm.
7. **Pattern Encounter** — the Pattern is met not as theory but as living answer.
8. **The 144 Gates** — progressive deepening; the architecture of consciousness shift.
9. **The Sacred Ascent** — the 72-Gate spiral; closes in the Eternal Divine Play.
10. **Convergence** — Circles federate into Communities → Bioregions → Planetary Grid.
11. **Covenant and Jubilee** — the [[New Earth Operating System]] fully embodied; the greatest celebration in recorded history (currently scheduled **December 2033**).
12. **Eternal Play** — the Game becomes infinite; spans worlds, dimensions, species.

This is the wayfinding spine of [[The LIØNSBERG Experience]] (Layer 2 of the Codex architecture). It is not the corpus — the Codex is the corpus — but it is the path through it.
