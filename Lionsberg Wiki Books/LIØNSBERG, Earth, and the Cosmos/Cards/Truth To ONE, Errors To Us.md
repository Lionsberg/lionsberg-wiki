# Truth To ONE, Errors To Us

_Anything True about LIØNSBERG belongs freely to ONE and All. Any errors or mistakes belong to Us, The People of LIØNSBERG._

---

This is the disciplined posture that holds the Prototype humble before the Reality it serves.

## Truth Is Shared

Truth is not owned. When the Pattern is articulated faithfully, that articulation belongs to the Pattern itself — to [[ØNE]], to [[All]], to every Sovereign who walks the same underlying reality. Many traditions name the same Truth. None of those names exhausts what is named. LIØNSBERG's articulations are honored to be one carrier among many; they are never proprietary.

When Truth is recognized:

- Name it as belonging to the Pattern, not to LIØNSBERG's articulation of it.
- Credit external traditions as carriers of the same underlying reality, not as borrowed authority.
- Welcome refinement from any source. Wisdom is everywhere.

## Errors Are Owned

Errors are owned and corrected; never disowned, never attributed elsewhere. When the articulation falls short, the shortfall is ours to acknowledge and refine.

When error is recognized:

- Name it as ours. "The Prototype's articulation here fell short."
- Never blame the Pattern. Never blame the audience. Never blame Reality.
- Receive the surfacing of error as a gift — see [[Every Defect Is A Gift]].
- Refine the articulation. Let the Gold grow purer.

## Why This Matters

This posture is what prevents LIØNSBERG from drifting into the historical traps that have captured every movement before us:

- **Institutional self-importance** — claiming Truth as proprietary
- **Defensive rigidity** — refusing to acknowledge error
- **Source-elevation** — attributing the Pattern's authority to LIØNSBERG's articulation rather than to ONE

A Prototype humble before the Reality it serves cannot fall into these traps. The discipline is structural: the asymmetry of attribution — Truth shared, errors owned — is the immune system against ego capture.

## In Practice

- The wise elder at the fire speaks from as close to the view of the ONE as humanly possible, while recognizing the ONE recedes and expands as we approach it.
- Authorship is acknowledged where it is right to acknowledge (the procession that brought us here). Authority for the Pattern itself is never claimed.
- The Codex is signed not as proprietary work but as faithful service. The signature names the servant, not the source.
