# Transform Weaponry to Livingry

Buckminster Fuller named the move. The Codex makes it operational.

**Weaponry** — the technologies, industries, infrastructures, and institutions organized for destruction, kontrolle, dominance. The submarines, the missiles, the surveillance systems, the engineered scarcity systems, the resource extraction machinery.

**Livingry** — the technologies, industries, infrastructures, and institutions organized for the flourishing of Life. The food sovereignty systems, the regenerative agriculture, the watershed restoration, the open energy commons, the healing arts at scale.

The same intelligence, the same engineering capacity, the same human labor pool, the same financial flows that currently produce Weaponry can — through redirection — produce Livingry instead. The math is the same; the orientation is different.

The transformation is **already underway** in fragments — every veteran who turns from war to building, every defense engineer who shifts to clean energy, every weapons-budget redirected to schools or hospitals or watershed restoration. The Codex's contribution is to **name the move at planetary scale**, give it operational coherence, and accelerate it through [[Swords to Ploughshares|the Trillion-Dollar Reallocation]].

The transformation has structural requirements:

- **Honored transition** — those who currently work in Weaponry are not the enemy. Many are trapped by economic necessity. The transition must include their dignified reskilling, redeployment, and welcoming into Livingry work.
- **Receivership of the Giants** — the major weapons-producing corporations either transform into Livingry-producing entities or are wound down with their assets redeployed to atonement-and-restoration.
- **Re-skilling at scale** — the engineering schools, the manufacturing infrastructure, the supply chains all bend toward life-supporting outputs.
- **Mythic re-narration** — the story of the heroic Warrior shifts from "one who destroys the enemy" to "[[The Sacred Warrior|one who protects life]]." The Warrior remains; the orientation transforms.

This is **the central operational move** of [[The 10 Year Grand Strategy]]. Without it, the Old World economy continues producing Weaponry until it bankrupts itself or destroys the planet. With it, the same capacity becomes the engine of [[The Hub Network Topology|the Hub Network]] and the regenerative economies of every Pillar.
