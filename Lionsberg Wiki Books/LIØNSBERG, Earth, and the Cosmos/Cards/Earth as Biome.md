# Earth as Biome

_The planet itself is a Biome — a single integrated living unit. One body, one atmosphere, one water cycle, one biosphere — the whole Earth as a single Sovereign body in the cosmic ecology._

---

The conventional understanding of "biome" names sub-planetary ecological zones — temperate forest, tropical rainforest, tundra, grassland, desert, ocean. **The deeper truth is that Earth itself is a Biome** — the integrated planetary system in which all sub-biomes participate, breathe together, exchange materials, and co-evolve.

**Earth is one body**:

- One **atmosphere** circulating around the whole sphere
- One **water cycle** moving from ocean to cloud to land to river to ocean
- One **biosphere** of interconnected life from microbe to whale
- One **geomagnetic field** generated by the Earth's iron core, shielding all life
- One **carbon cycle**, one **nitrogen cycle**, one **water cycle** — none stop at any boundary
- One **gravitational and rotational reality** — the day, the night, the seasons all planetary

[[ØNE]] Body composed of countless living cells, organs, and systems. Gaia. Pachamama. The Mother. Names from every tradition for the same recognition: this whole planet is alive, and we are within Her.

## Stewarding Earth-as-Biome

To recognize Earth as a single Biome is to recognize that no Bioregion or Community or Sovereign acts in isolation from the Whole. Every Watershed feeds an ocean. Every breath shared in the atmosphere. Every climate decision a planetary act.

The work at every smaller scale must honor the Earth-Biome as ground:

- The Carbon cycle is a planetary concern, addressed locally
- The Water cycle is a planetary concern, addressed by Watershed
- The Atmosphere is shared — what one Bioregion emits, all Bioregions breathe
- The Biosphere is one — the extinction of species anywhere is the impoverishment of the Whole

**The Earth Biome is what we are co-stewarding when we say "co-create Heaven On Earth."**
