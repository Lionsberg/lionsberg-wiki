# LUV — LIONSBERG Units of Value

The economic protocol of the New Civilization.

**LUV** (LIONSBERG Units of Value) and the broader system of **Interoperable Local CurrentSees** is the economic substrate by which value flows through the Body of LIØNSBERG without recreating the extraction economies of the Old World.

The principle: **value flows toward the work that serves life**.

Operational features (to be articulated through Phase 2-3 work and the audit of [[Superorganism-Earth]] and [[LIONSBERG FSC Pattern Language]]):

- **Proof-of-contribution**, not proof-of-stake or proof-of-work-as-extraction.
- **Local CurrentSees** that circulate within Communities and Bioregions, interoperable across the broader Federation but not extractable by distant capital.
- **Compressed ratios** — the FSC tradition names 7:1 as the maximum ratio of compensation within a Federation. The principle: dignity of labor is preserved; concentration of wealth is structurally prevented.
- **Tithe to the Commons** — a portion of every flow returns to the shared infrastructure, the land, the next generation, the [[Sacred Shared Infrastructure]].
- **Sacred Reciprocity** — every exchange honors [[Right Relationship and Sacred Reciprocity]]. Gift and trade are both present. Pure extraction is structurally rejected.

The LUV system replaces neither human labor markets nor the gift economy of healthy traditional cultures. It is the connective tissue that allows both to operate inside a federation that does not collapse into either coerced commodification or destructive accumulation.

This concept lives canonically inside [[The LIØNSBERG Pattern Language]] (Volume III) — specifically within the **Economy and Stewardship** element of the Pattern.
