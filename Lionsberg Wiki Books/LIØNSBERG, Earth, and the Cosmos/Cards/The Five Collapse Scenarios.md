# The Five Collapse Scenarios

The named futures, weighted by probability.

The word "collapse" is too vague to act on. _Which collapse? How bad? How fast?_ Different Pilgrims hear different things. The Five Collapse Scenarios concretize what "collapse" could actually look like — five distinct futures, each with named characteristics, weighted by reasoned probability.

The Pilgrim does not need to predict which scenario will occur. The Pilgrim needs to **see all five clearly** so that whatever does occur is recognizable, and so that preparation can be informed rather than panicked.

**Scenario 1 — Localized Crises (~20%)**

The least severe outcome. Regional collapses occur — specific countries fail, specific bioregions face acute food/water/energy crises, specific cities become unlivable — but the global system continues. Localized refugee flows. Localized humanitarian catastrophes. Localized authoritarianism in places that fail. The Old World holds together at the planetary scale even as it breaks in places. Mortality: tens of millions to low hundreds of millions over the decade, concentrated in the affected regions. Ecological loss: severe in affected zones, moderate globally.

This is the "everything will be fine for most" scenario — fine for the Pilgrim's specific location, devastating for the people in the regions that collapsed.

**Scenario 2 — Widespread Social Unrest (~30%)**

The most likely. Civil disorder, contested elections, mass demonstrations, paramilitary movements, regional conflicts across multiple continents. The institutions hold formally but lose practical legitimacy across large fractions of their populations. Authoritarian governments tighten control; democratic governments fail to govern. Supply chains intermittent; economies in chronic stress. Mortality: hundreds of millions cumulatively, mostly indirect (medical-system stress, infrastructure failures, conflict casualties, deaths-of-despair). Ecological loss: continuing severe.

This is the "everyone feels it but most survive" scenario.

**Scenario 3 — Global Humanitarian Crisis (~25%)**

Severe. Multiple systemic failures cascade — financial collapse triggers food-system failure triggers refugee flows triggers political collapse in multiple regions. The global supply chain breaks for months at a time. Pharmaceutical access fails. Banking systems become unreliable. Travel becomes dangerous. Authoritarian "solutions" proliferate. Mortality: 500 million to 1 billion cumulatively over the decade. Ecological loss: catastrophic for most ecosystems; mass extinction event accelerates.

This is the "civilization breaks but does not end" scenario.

**Scenario 4 — Near-Total Collapse (~15%)**

Devastating. Cascading failures across all major systems. Population centers without reliable food/water/medicine. Mass migration on the scale of billions. State actors fail; non-state actors fill power vacuums. Nuclear and biological weapon proliferation. Mass die-off through famine, disease, conflict. Mortality: 2-4 billion cumulatively over the decade. Ecological loss: most planetary ecosystems destabilized to the point of regime-shift.

This is the "civilization mostly ends; surviving pockets begin rebuilding" scenario.

**Scenario 5 — Extinction-Level Event (~10%)**

Worst case. Triggered by some combination of: full nuclear exchange, engineered bioweapon release, runaway AI misalignment, cascading ecological tipping points, off-world actor intervention at maximum severity. Most of humanity perishes. Most surviving populations live in conditions analogous to early-medieval subsistence. Earth's habitable zones contract dramatically. Mortality: 5-7+ billion. Ecological loss: extinction event comparable to the largest in geological history.

This is the "humanity nearly ends; the species itself is at risk" scenario.

**Reading the scenarios:**

The probabilities are **reasoned estimates from a serious analyst working from current evidence**. They are not predictions. The Pilgrim is invited to reweight them according to their own analysis (see [[The Probability Calculation Method]]). The point is not to settle on one set of numbers; the point is to **make the scenarios real enough to plan against**.

Note: the cumulative probability of scenarios 3-5 (the seriously catastrophic outcomes) is **~50%**. This is the number that should sit at the front of every decision the awakened Pilgrim makes. A 50% probability of catastrophic collapse over the coming decade is **roughly the same probability as a coin flip**. No rational being plans their life on the assumption that a coin flip will land favorably.

The scenarios also clarify what the [[The 10 Year Grand Strategy|10-Year Grand Strategy]] is responding to. The Strategy is not animated by certainty of doom; it is animated by **the rational response to a coin-flip probability of catastrophe combined with the [[The Asymmetry of Consequences|asymmetry of consequences]]**. Even in the 20% scenario (Localized Crises), the work of building the New Civilization is valuable. In the 30% scenario (Widespread Social Unrest), the work is essential. In the 25% scenario (Global Humanitarian Crisis), the work is the only hope of preserving civilizational continuity. In the 15% and 10% scenarios, the work is the rebuild infrastructure for whatever surviving humanity must reconstruct from.

**Across all five scenarios, the response is the same**: build the New, in parallel, while there is still time. The work succeeds in every scenario. The work fails only if it is not undertaken.

A Pilgrim sitting with the scenarios is often shaken by them. The shaking is appropriate. The discipline is not to suppress the shake; the discipline is to **use the shake** as the energy for the [[The Voluntary Leap Forward|Voluntary Leap Forward]]. The body's recognition that something serious is at stake is part of how the Pilgrim crosses the threshold from spectator to participant.

For the Pilgrim sharing the scenarios with others: do not exaggerate them. Do not soften them. Read them aloud. Let the listener sit with them. Then ask: _"What does this ask of you?"_ The listener's own answer is the beginning of their awakening.
