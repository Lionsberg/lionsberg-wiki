# The Pattern Governs, Not People

Every long-lived voluntary movement has discovered the same structural truth.

**The Rule governs the abbot.** Even the Benedictine head of a monastery is bound by the Rule of Benedict, not above it. The Rule is what the community gathers around. The abbot is a steward of the Rule, not its author.

**The Twelve Traditions govern AA.** No CEO. No bishop. No board with veto power. The Traditions are read aloud at every meeting. They govern the meeting; the meeting governs itself by reading them.

**The Great Law of Peace governs the chiefs.** The Iroquois chiefs hold their seats only as long as they uphold the Great Law. They are removable when they violate it. The Law is sovereign.

**The GPL governs the code.** Linux has no king. The license is sovereign. Every contributor is bound by it, including Linus.

This is the structural insight that lets movements scale without collapsing into personality cults or institutional ossification: **the Pattern is what governs, and every member is bound by it — including any apparent leader.**

The corollary: **whoever places themselves above the Pattern has, in that moment, left the Movement.** Restoration requires return to obedience to the Pattern, not negotiation with the apparent leader's exemption.

This is why LIØNSBERG is **not a religion of personality**. The Codex is what governs. Whoever lives the Codex is in. Whoever places themselves above it is, by their own act, outside.
