# The Hub Network Topology

The physical infrastructure topology of the New Civilization, articulated as a federated network of Hubs at every scale.

The topology nests:

- **Core Hub** — one, planetary
- **Georegional Hubs** — distributed across major continental zones
- **Bioregional Hubs** — one per substantial watershed / ecosystem cluster
- **Ecoregional Hubs** — finer-grained ecological zones
- **City Hubs** — within major urban centers
- **Local Hubs** — neighborhood / district scale
- **Community Hubs** — at the ~10,000-person scale where [[The First Three Percent and Fibonacci Spread|The 300]] operate
- **Micro Hubs** — even smaller, embedded directly in neighborhoods

Each Hub at each scale carries the **same DNA** — the Twelve Elements operate at every level — adapted to the specific scale and place. A Bioregional Hub is structurally homologous to a Community Hub, just larger.

Each Hub is **physical** — actual buildings, actual land, actual people. Existing infrastructure (libraries, schools, community centers, monasteries, abandoned commercial spaces) is repurposed where possible. New construction follows Sacred Architecture (Pillar IX).

Each Hub is **a node of [[The Sovereign Superorganism|the Sovereign Superorganism]]** — sovereign within its scale, federated outward by mutual consent, never subordinated.

The total budget topology (per The Lionsberg Strategy and Plan of Action) aggregates to approximately $15-20 trillion across the full Hub Network — the redirection target for [[Swords to Ploughshares|the Swords-to-Ploughshares reallocation]] of military spending.

The Hub Network is **how Heaven on Earth gets built physically**. Without the Hubs, the Pattern stays in conversation. With the Hubs, the Pattern stands in the land, becomes a place beings can come to, becomes the visible alternative the awakening can see and join.

Lives canonically in [[The Great Game of LIØNSBERG]] (Volume III) and [[The LIØNSBERG Playbook]] (Volume IV) as the physical implementation infrastructure.
