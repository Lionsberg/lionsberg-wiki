# Heaven and Hell as Co-Created Trajectories

The theological correction at the heart of the New Story.

Heaven and Hell are not metaphysical destinations awarded after death by an external judge. Heaven and Hell are **trajectories** — directions of co-creation that Beings, Communities, and Civilizations move along, here, now.

A trajectory is the integral of choices. Every choice for Love, Truth, Justice, Beauty, Stewardship, Wisdom contributes to the **Heavenward vector**. Every choice for kontrolle, deceit, predation, ugliness, extraction, foolishness contributes to the **Hellward vector**. The sum across a life, a Circle, a community, a Civilization is the world that is being co-created — and inhabited.

Earth is a **co-creative laboratory** in which trajectories of every direction are being run simultaneously. At every scale:

- A **soul** is running a Heavenward or Hellward trajectory through its daily choices.
- A **family** is running one or the other through the patterns it transmits to its children.
- A **community** is running one through its agreements, its rhythms, its tolerances.
- A **Civilization** is running one through its institutions, its incentives, its myths.

The reality of Heaven or Hell is felt **here, now, in the lived texture of life**. A community running a Heavenward trajectory feels different to enter — the air is different, the children's eyes are different, the elders are honored, the food is whole. A community running a Hellward trajectory feels different too — and we have all walked into both kinds.

Heaven On Earth is therefore not a fantasy. It is the **achievable terminus** of a Heavenward trajectory pursued long enough, by enough Beings, in enough places, working together. Hell on Earth is the achievable terminus of the opposite — and parts of Earth are demonstrating this terminus in real time. The trajectories produce their terminuses, given time.

Three implications of this seeing:

- **The choice is not whether to believe in Heaven or Hell.** The choice is which trajectory we are running, today, this hour, in this decision. Belief follows trajectory. The Heavenward life produces Heavenward belief; the Hellward life produces Hellward belief. The doing is the proof.
- **Death does not award the destination.** Death is a passage. What we have become along the trajectory is what we carry. The Heavenward being meets Death and continues onward in the same direction at a different layer. The Hellward being meets Death and continues onward in the same direction at a different layer. The trajectory does not pause for the passage; the architecture is continuous.
- **Co-creation is total.** No one is given Heaven or Hell. Everyone is co-creating one or the other — alone and together. The ONE does not impose; the ONE invites. The choosing is ours. The terminus is what we have made.

This is the **Theological Inversion** the New Story performs. The Old Story said: _Heaven and Hell are out there; behave so you reach the right one._ The New Story says: _Heaven and Hell are trajectories you are running right now; the doing is the destination._

[[The Great Game of LIØNSBERG|The Great Game]] is the operational expression of choosing the Heavenward trajectory at scale. Every Quest, every Circle, every Hub, every Pillar is one more vector contributing to the Heavenward sum. The Pattern itself is the **angle of ascent**.

Lives canonically in [[The Story of LIØNSBERG]] (Volume II) as a foundational theological articulation, and reverberates through every Volume — for the Pattern of the Codex is, ultimately, the angle of Heaven applied to every domain.
