# The Greatest Jubilee

The greatest celebration and Jubilee in history is **not a fixed date.** It is the state-of-system event that dawns when **the last cohort of Earth-Sovereigns completes [[The 144 Gates of Initiation]] and stands at Blue Belt in [[The Way Of The One Body]]** — when no one is still asleep in the Caves, and every Sovereign on Earth is participating consciously and competently in the co-creation of Heaven Here and Nøw.

## Movement 10 Of The 144 Gates

The Greatest Jubilee is Movement 10 of The 144 Gates. It opens when Movement 9 — [[The Great ReFounding]] — completes: that is, when the final cohort crosses the threshold. From the moment that last Sovereign is recognized, the species enters the Jubilee.

## State Of The System, Not Calendar Entry

Any date previously held as the Jubilee anchor — _e.g._ [[Covenant and Jubilee — December 2033]] — is one possible projection of when this might land. The deeper truth: **the Jubilee arrives when the last Sovereign crosses into participation. Not before. Not by calendar.**

## What Unfolds

When recognition that the last has crossed is acknowledged, the species' wilderness ends. Memory is healed across generations. The exiled return. All kin reconcile — with each other, with the land, with all the relations. Debts are forgiven across the Body. Earth-wide celebration unfolds at every scale. The Wedding of Heaven and Earth is enacted. Generations are anointed. Gifts distributed. The New Dawn rises — Earth recognized as fully participating member of the wider cosmic Body. The Greatest Jubilee is sealed in Earth's living memory, and Movement 11 — millennia of co-creating Heaven — opens.
