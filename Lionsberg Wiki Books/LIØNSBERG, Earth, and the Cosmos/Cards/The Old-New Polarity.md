# The Old/New Polarity

The signature rhetorical engine of LIØNSBERG discourse:

> **Old: {what is collapsing}.**
> **New: {what is being co-created}.**

Every Thesis follows this form. Every Pillar uses it. The 99 Theses of The Great UnVeiling use it. The 144 Theses across the 12 Pillars use it. The 172 numbered theses across the populated domains of [[The Theses of LIØNSBERG]] use it.

The Old/New Polarity is **not a complaint about what was.** It is **a precise naming of the inflection**: this dies; that lives; here is what passes; here is what is born.

The Old/New Polarity is **not nostalgia** for an earlier purer Old. The Old is the long pattern of kontrolle, extraction, separation, and forgetting. There is no return to a better version of it.

The Old/New Polarity is **not utopia** about an idealized New. The New is concrete, livable, already partially instantiated wherever Circles meet, wherever Quests are run, wherever Heaven is co-created on Earth right now.

The polarity is **the structure of becoming.** Every transformation walks through it. Every awakening recognizes it. Every Circle articulates its own Old/New polarities for the specific work it does.

Examples in the operational register:

> Old: Rote memorization. New: Deep wisdom and understanding.
> Old: Debt-based money. New: Asset-and-value-backed currencies.
> Old: Extractive industries. New: Regenerative industries.
> Old: Empire by Force. New: Federation by mutual consent.
> Old: Anti-Goodness. New: [[Right Relationship and Sacred Reciprocity]].

Lives canonically in [[The LIØNSBERG Lexicon]] as the foundational rhetorical form, with cross-reference to every Volume that uses it.
