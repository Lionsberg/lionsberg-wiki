# The Sacred Onboarding Arc

The path by which a Pilgrim becomes a Citizen.

The onboarding arc is **not** a corporate orientation. It is **not** a course or a training. It is the **sacred passage** through which a being who has received the Seed becomes a Co-Creator of the New Civilization.

The arc moves through:

**Sacred Invitation.** Someone the Pilgrim trusts hands them the Seed in the right register — not as marketing, not as recruitment, but as honest naming of what is being co-created and what the Pilgrim's resonance might bring to it.

**Expression of Interest & Mutual Exploration.** The Pilgrim and the inviting Circle explore the resonance together. Is there alignment of Spirit? Is the work theirs to take up? No pressure, no judgment if the answer is "not now." Many will need to circle back later.

**Sacred Onboarding.** When alignment is clear, the Pilgrim enters formation: foundational curriculum (Story, Pattern, Game), mentorship pairing, integration practices (meditation, breathwork, movement, martial arts), and community immersion. Not a download. A relational, embodied, slow opening.

**Sacred Covenant Ceremony.** When the Pilgrim is ready, they enter into Covenant. Sacred space, vows spoken aloud, symbolic acts (rings, oils, fires, sealings), witnessed by the Community, celebrated in joy. This is the threshold. Crossing is voluntary, irreversible in spirit, honored by all present.

**Integration Period.** The first ninety days. Weekly check-ins, integration workbook, monthly Circle of Reflection. The vows are tested in the actual conditions of life. Any drift is caught early; any need is held.

**The Tracks.** From there, the Pilgrim enters **the 3-Year Foundations** — the formation cycle empirically required for a being to feel fully free, empowered, and self-governing in the new pattern — and then optionally the **7-Year Mastery** path for those called to deeper service.

**Holonic Citizenship.** At the completion of the 3-Year Foundations, the Pilgrim — now a Citizen — is welcomed into full standing within the Body. The citizenship ceremony marks not the end of the journey but its true beginning.

This entire arc maps directly onto [[The 12 Stages of the Sovereign's Path]] — Stages 4 through 10 are the Sacred Onboarding Arc, lived in sequence.
