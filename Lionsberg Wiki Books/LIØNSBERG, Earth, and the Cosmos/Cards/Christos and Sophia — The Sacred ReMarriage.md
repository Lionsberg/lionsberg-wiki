# Christos and Sophia — The Sacred ReMarriage

The cosmic reunion of the Divine Masculine and the Divine Feminine.

**Christos** — the Divine Masculine principle. The Logos. The structuring Word. The active fire that crystallizes Form. The shepherd, the warrior, the king, the builder. Not gender-male as it is reduced in the Old World; the **archetypal Masculine** of which gender-male is one local expression.

**Sophia** — the Divine Feminine principle. The Wisdom. The receptive sea from which Form rises. The mother, the lover, the priestess, the healer. Not gender-female as the Old World reduces; the **archetypal Feminine** of which gender-female is one local expression.

The Old World **severed** Christos and Sophia. The result has been visible across millennia: religions that worship a male God without His consort; philosophies that elevate reason without intuition; cultures that honor activity without receptivity; sciences that dissect without listening; civilizations that build without nurturing. The wound is cosmic-scale.

The Sacred ReMarriage is **the reunion** — not of human marriage at the social scale (though that is one local fruit), but of the **archetypal principles themselves**:

- In every being, masculine and feminine restored to balance
- In every Circle, both registers honored
- In every institution, both principles operating
- In the Cosmos itself, Christos and Sophia recognized as **two faces of the ONE** rather than as separated principalities

The ReMarriage is part of [[Covenant and Jubilee — December 2033]] at the archetypal scale. The Old World's severance dissolves; the New Civilization restores the original union.

This is not metaphor about human relationships. It is the cosmological condition that conditions human relationships, civilizational forms, religious imagery, scientific method, political structure. Restoration here ripples through everything else.

Lives canonically in [[The Story of LIØNSBERG]] as the archetypal layer of the cosmic Story.
