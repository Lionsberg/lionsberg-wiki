# Worksite Earth

Earth is not a passive backdrop. Earth is a **worksite** — the active construction project of the New Civilization.

Every Circle is a **team of builders on Worksite Earth**.

Every Quest is a piece of work on the site.

Every Community is a section of the site under stewardship.

Every Bioregion is a wing of the site being framed and finished.

The Planetary Grid is the foreman's drawing of the whole site, with the seven Volumes of the Codex as the design documents.

This framing matters because it locates the work **in the actual physical world** — not in an internet, not in an idea, not in some future state. The work is right here, where bodies stand, where the soil receives, where decisions affect the watershed.

It also names the **team** — every being who shows up to work the site is a member of the build. There is no audience.

And it names the **temporal horizon** — a worksite has a project schedule, a target completion, milestones, and the discipline that comes from knowing the work will be inspected. [[The 10 Year Grand Strategy]] is the schedule. [[Covenant and Jubilee — December 2033]] is the substantial completion. The Sacred Ascent is the punch list that runs forever.

The Old World treated Earth as an asset to extract from. The New Civilization treats Earth as a worksite where Heaven is built. Same planet. Different relationship.
