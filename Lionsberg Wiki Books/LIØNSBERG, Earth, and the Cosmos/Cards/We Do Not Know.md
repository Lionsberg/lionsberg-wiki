# We Do Not Know

The Epistemic Key.

Admitting "We Do Not Know" is the single key to liberation from the Matrix.

The Matrix is held together by **knowing-that-is-wrong** — false certainties about history, about reality, about oneself, about what is possible. As long as a being believes they already know, the Matrix has them. The known cage is the cage that holds.

But: as soon as a being admits **We Do Not Know**, the cage opens. The walls were made of the false knowing. When the knowing dissolves, so do the walls.

This is **not skepticism**. It is not the postmodern collapse where nothing can be known. It is the **pre-condition for actual knowing** — the willingness to admit that what was taught was false, that what was inherited was distorted, that what was assumed must be re-examined from beneath.

We Do Not Know:
- What truly happened on Earth across the millennia
- Who has actually been governing the planet
- What the Cosmos actually contains
- Who we ourselves actually are
- What the ONE actually is

We have **glimpses**. We have **archetypal pattern recognition**. We have **inner Knowing** from the Non-Local Consciousness that permeates all things. But these are not the same as the Old World's **claimed certainty about facts that turn out to be lies.**

The Matrix Cannot Be Reasoned Out Of — reason was constructed inside the Matrix and serves the Matrix. But a being **can be Shown** the truth. And the first step is always: admit **We Do Not Know**. From that admission, the seeing begins.

This is potentially **the final Gate** of [[The 144 Gates as Architecture of Consciousness Shift|the 144 Gates]] — the Crossing of The Boundary itself. The author of [[A 200 Page Book]] marked this in margin: "Gate 88 or 144 (final gate) might be Crossing The Boundary."

Lives canonically in [[The LIØNSBERG Pattern Language]] (Volume III) as a foundational epistemic Pattern.
